FEATURES

uMobix is a trustworthy monitoring program that can keep track of your child's online activities without their knowing. Aside from that, it's useful for keeping track of your employees, elderly parents, or anyone else. Let's go through some of the features that make this one of the best parental control software options for 2025.

  • Call Monitoring - This feature allows parents to remotely monitor their child's call history and contacts on mobile devices running uMobix. Every call received, its duration, and whether it is accepted or rejected is displayed. uMobix syncs with the smartphone every 5 minutes, so you're always up to date on your child's phone call activity.

  • SMS Monitoring - SMS tracking is also possible with uMobix monitoring tools. Like the Call Tracking tool, you may analyze every incoming and outgoing message and keep an eye on your child's contacts. You stay connected to the target device with regular syncing. To be sure nothing is missed, you can also check deleted messages.

  • Social Media Apps Monitoring- One of the key benefits of this program is the large number of social networking apps it can monitor. You can monitor the most popular social networking and instant messaging apps, such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. You can also keep note of when the app was closed and the total time spent. This should give you an indication of how much screen time your youngster spends.

  • Mobile Contacts Monitoring - By granting complete, unfettered access to the target device's address book, uMobix makes it simpler for parents to check their kids' contact lists.

  • GPS Tracking - This functionality allows your phone to function as a mobile tracker. It uses the remote device's geolocation mechanism to relay data to uMobix in real time. This allows you to keep track of where your child goes when they are using their gadget. In addition, you will be able to locate them quickly in an emergency. Besides their physical location, this feature also provides a timeline of places they've been, coordinates, addresses, and map views.

  • Keylogger - To enhance your ability to track your child's phone, uMobix offers a keylogger feature. As the name suggests, a keylogger records every keystroke made on the target device and keeps a log of them. You may learn more about what your children are saying, searching for, and sending online with a keylogger. You'll also be able to see any accounts they may have created without your consent and any messages they may have held off sending.

  • App Usage Monitoring - Are you concerned that your kid spends too much time on their phone? With this feature, you will know exactly what apps are installed on the phone and the frequency and time spent on these apps. You can also choose to delete an app or block it altogether.

  • Spyware Scanner - uMobix does much more than just letting you monitor and track your children's devices. It also offers protection against malware such as viruses, trojans, and other threats! With this feature, you can detect and delete malicious spyware that your kids may have unwittingly installed or downloaded on their phones.
