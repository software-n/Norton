FEATURES

Eyezy boasts an impressive array of features that make it a valuable parental control software solution:

  • Website/Wi-Fi blocker - Eyezy's Website/Wi-Fi blocker feature empowers you, as a parent, to elevate online safety for your children by granting you precise control over internet access. With this tool, you have the ability to block access to specific websites, whether it's adult content or distracting platforms during study hours, ensuring a safer and more focused online experience for your child. Moreover, the capacity to block Wi-Fi networks adds an extra layer of control, enabling you to manage access on devices that connect to multiple networks and safeguarding against unsecured or public networks. Supported by time-based rules and detailed reporting, Eyezy provides a comprehensive solution tailored to parents seeking to establish a secure digital environment for their children.

  • Connection Blocker - With Connection Blocker, you gain the ability to both monitor and manage your child's online activities. This feature empowers you to oversee the websites they can access, the apps they use, and the duration of their usage, ensuring a safe and balanced digital experience.

  • Plans Breaker - This feature lets you gain access to your child's social calendar, allowing you to stay in the loop about their activities and the individuals they're engaging with. It provides a convenient means to prevent them from participating in events that may not align with your approval and values, giving you a proactive approach to their social interactions and well-being.

  • Keystroke Capture - Eyezy's Keystroke Capture feature covertly records every keystroke made on the target phone, accomplished through a keylogger and by capturing screenshots whenever social media or messaging apps are accessed.

  • Pinpoint - With Pinpoint, you have the ability to precisely locate your child and track their movements. This feature provides you with their exact location on a map, thanks to GPS location tracking and advanced WiFi network analysis. Stay informed about where they are and where they've been, ensuring their safety and your peace of mind.

  • Location tracking & geofencing - With Location tracking & geofencing, you can ensure your child's safety by closely monitoring their whereabouts. Whether you want to track their journey home from school or verify their location during social outings, this feature provides you with real-time insights. Geofencing adds an extra layer of security by allowing you to define safe zones and receive instant notifications if your child ventures beyond these predefined boundaries, offering you enhanced peace of mind as a caregiver.

  • Magic Alerts - Magic Alerts empower you to establish boundaries and receive timely notifications. Eyezy keeps you informed if your child engages in controversial or inappropriate conversations, searches for sensitive content, or ventures to places they shouldn't be. It's an intelligent and proactive tool that keeps you in the know about your child's digital and physical activities.

  • Social Spotlight - Social Spotlight provides you with an all-encompassing view of your child's digital interactions. With this feature, you can access their private text conversations, even those they may have deleted. It goes a step further by allowing you to monitor messages across popular social media apps like WhatsApp, KiK, Tinder, Snapchat, Skype, Instagram, Facebook, and more. Additionally, you have the capability to record their screen, ensuring you remain informed and equipped to nurture a secure and responsible online presence for your child. Quite impressive, isn't it?

  • Files Finder - Files Finder provides you with a comprehensive view of the content stored on their phone. It neatly organizes pictures, videos, and files, making it effortless to access all their data in one convenient location.

  • Phone Analyzer - Phone Analyzer provides you with detailed insights into their phone usage. You can easily access information about their outgoing and incoming calls, call durations, and much more, ensuring you have a comprehensive understanding of their communication patterns.

  • Web Magnifier - See what they’re searching for on the internet, what websites they visit, what they’ve got bookmarked, and their interests.

  • Keyword Tracker - With Eyezy's Keyword tracker, you receive alerts whenever your child types prohibited words into their device. This tool allows you to maintain a watchful eye on their digital communication, ensuring their online safety and promoting responsible language usage.

  • Invisible Shield - Invisible Shield grants you the ability to discreetly monitor their activities without detection. With Eyezy's hidden functionality, you can stay informed without them being aware of your presence, ensuring a subtle yet effective approach to their digital supervision.


    • COMPATIBLE DEVICES

    You can install eyeZy on various platforms, including:

    Android: Eyezy is compatible with most Android phones and tablets. However, the device you want to track with eyeZy should be running at least Android 4.0. If the phone is not rooted, certain features will have limitations. Specifically, you won't be able to use Instant Messenger tracking, keylogging, gather information on Wi-Fi networks, or monitor their Gmail account. Other features may also have reduced functionality. Rooting your Android device involves linking it to a desktop computer and following specific instructions to replace the operating system. Eyezy provides a step-by-step guide, and their 24/7 support team can offer further technical assistance if needed. Regardless of rooting status, you will require physical access to the device to install the app, and it must have internet access for tracking to work.

    iOS: Eyezy can be installed on any iPhone with at least iOS 7.0. Similar to Android, some features of the app require jailbreaking the phone. However, unlike Android, you do not need physical access to the device for installation. If you have the iCloud login details for the device, you can perform a data-sync from Eyezy without being near the phone. Keep in mind that this method does not provide instant updates for keylogging or location data. For up-to-date tracking, manual installation of the Eyezy app is recommended for full device coverage. An internet connection is necessary to send data updates to Eyezy

    Windows & Mac OSX: Currently, Eyezy offers its tracking package exclusively for mobile devices. However, you can access the Eyezy tracker dashboard from any device with a compatible browser, including Edge, Safari, or other third-party browsers. Eyezy offers local Wi-Fi data sync as an installation method, requiring you to link your target device to a computer for the initial install. Alternatively, you can download the Eyezy app without the need for computer access. If you wish to install Eyezy without an internet connection, you can connect your Android device to a computer and transfer the .apk install file via the connection. For iPhone devices, a similar process is possible, but you'll need to install iTunes first before sending the file to the phone.
