FEATURES

Since its initial release in 2010, mSpy has quickly become one of the most well-known and frequently utilized parental control programs in the world. Among other things, the software provides clients with a transparent analysis of smartphone usage. Furthermore, it is dependable, simple to set up, and compatible with both iOS and Android devices.

All data collected by the app is sent to the company's cloud server, which you may view via the web dashboard. Speaking of dashboards, it's really one of mSpy's standout features. It makes it extremely simple to remotely operate your child's phone and monitor all the data it collects. Other parental control programs need you to scroll before reaching the information you seek. Not with mSpy. The dashboard is well-organized, and the main page includes an overall summary.

Now, let’s get on with the features. Here’s what you can do with mSpy:

  • Monitor and Restrict Websites - This feature lets you keep tabs on all the websites your child or target user has been visiting, including any pages they've bookmarked. Additionally, the software can notify you immediately when a certain keyword is entered into a mobile device. Such functionality can be immensely helpful for parents and allow them to better understand what their kids are searching for and watching on the Internet. If the person you're monitoring is using popular web browsers like Chrome or Safari, you can also ban specific websites.

  • Monitor call history - Check the target's incoming and outgoing calls, name and number, as well as the date, time, and duration of the calls.

  • Track emails - Using mSpy, you can monitor all incoming and outgoing email messages right on mSpy's dashboard. Find out about the receiver/sender's email address, the time as well as the content of the email. This information may be retrieved, just like the SMS text log and call log details, even if the monitored individual has deleted their emails.

  • See pictures and videos - View every image and video your kids have on their phones.

  • Geofencing - With the most recent GPS technology, mSpy enables you to track your children's whereabouts. With geofencing, you can create an infinite number of "safe" or "restricted" areas on the map, each of which can span up to 6.21 miles or 9,999 meters. For example, you can set your home, school district, church, their grandparent's home, or community as safe zones. At the same time, you can set places that aren't kid-friendly as "restricted zones". If your child enters or leaves any of these defined areas, you will receive an alert.

  • Instant Messaging Apps Monitoring - Nowadays, kids are texting, instant messaging, or both. This means you can't just track text messages alone. Fortunately, mSpy lets you monitor popular messaging apps like Whatsapp, Skype, iMessage, Viber, Kik, Line, Snapchat, Tinder, Google Hangouts, and Telegram, just to name a few.

  • View Contact and Calendar Information - You can view the contact names, email addresses, phone numbers, and physical addresses saved on the target mobile phone using mSpy. You can also look at the target device's calendar schedule, which will help you keep track of any upcoming appointments, projects, and/or calendar entries.

  • Keyword alerts - With this alert tool, you can make a list of keywords that you want to be alerted to, like "drugs," "suicide", "sex," "nigger", and "guns." You will be notified by email when these words are used in texts, chats, emails, searches, etc.

    • These are just a few of the tools available from mSpy to keep your children safe online. Again, the best part is that your children will be completely unaware that they are being watched. Thanks to its stealth mode feature, it can run in the background without being detected: no alerts, app icons, or notifications!


    PRICING AND PLANS

    Despite having a wide range of functions, the tracking program is still considerably less expensive than many other phone spy apps. mSpy currently offers three premium packages:

  • 1 month – $48.99

  • 3 months – $27.99

  • 12 months – $11.66

    • Like any other subscription service, the longer your subscription is valid, the cheaper it costs. mSpy accepts popular payment methods, including Google and Apple Pay. They also have a very transparent refund policy. If, for any reason, you're not satisfied, you have 14 days to contact their billing department and get a refund.


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    In addition to help articles, mSpy provides three tiers of customer support. Customers who use the Basic and Premium programs from mSpy are eligible for the first level of support. They can send emails and support tickets, visit the self-service help area, and use the live chat feature, which is available round-the-clock. The second tier provides more personalized support, while the third level can provide professional assistance to those who need to jailbreak or root a phone and install the app remotely. Keep in mind, however, that both level 2 and level 3 are paid support.
