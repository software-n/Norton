FEATURES

Panda Dome Family is a comprehensive parental control software offering various features to help parents protect their children from online threats. It was developed by Panda Security, a cybersecurity company founded in 1990 that provides antivirus, firewall, and other security solutions for individuals and businesses. Some of the key features of Panda Dome Family include:

  • Website filtering - This feature allows you to control your child's access to the internet and restrict them from accessing inappropriate content.

    With Panda Dome's website filtering feature, you can create a blacklist or whitelist of websites that your child can or cannot access. The blacklist contains websites that you want to block, while the whitelist contains websites that you want to allow. You can set up rules to restrict access based on categories such as violence, adult content, drugs, and more.

    Panda Dome's website filtering feature also allows you to monitor your child's internet activity and block specific URLs. You can set up schedules to restrict access to certain websites during specific times of the day, such as during school hours or bedtime.

    Overall, Panda Dome's website filtering feature provides a comprehensive and customizable solution for parents who want to protect their children from the dangers of the internet.

  • Real-time GPS tracking - Real-time GPS tracking allows parents to always keep an eye on where their children are. Notifications can be set up for when the kids enter or leave specific areas, offering peace of mind in case of an emergency. For example, if a child goes missing or is in danger, parents can quickly locate them and provide help. This feature is invaluable for ensuring the safety and well-being of children.

  • App and game blocking - With this feature, you can control what apps and games can be accessed on your kids' devices. You can limit access to certain apps or games that you think are not suitable for your child or could be a distraction. This helps ensure that your kids are not spending too much time on activities that are not beneficial to their learning and instead encourages them to focus on their studies.

  • Panic button - Panda Dome's Panic Button is a safety feature that allows a child to send an immediate alert to their parent or guardian in case of an emergency. When activated, the Panic Button sends a notification to the parent's smartphone or email, indicating that the child needs help. This notification or alert will contain the child’s location information, allowing the parent to respond quickly and provide assistance. This feature is particularly useful for younger children who may not be able to communicate their location or situation effectively in an emergency.

  • Time management - With Time Management, you can set limits on the amount of time your kids spend on their devices. It also lets you create schedules for device usage, like allowing your child to use their device only during homework time or after-school activities. When the designated time period is over, the device will be locked automatically. This is a great way for parents to make sure their child isn't spending too much time on their device and to encourage a healthier balance between screen time and other activities such as outdoor play and socializing with friends and family.

  • Location history access - Location history access allows you to monitor the device's past locations over a certain period, giving you insight into where your child has been and helping you make sure that they are where they are supposed to be.

  • Monitoring - This feature gives parents insight into their child's online activity by providing detailed reports on the websites and applications used on the child's device. This helps ensure that your kids are using their computers, phones, and other devices safely and responsibly. Additionally, it can be useful for identifying potential risks and inappropriate behavior, such as accessing inappropriate content or interacting with strangers online.

  • Safe browsing - Panda Dome Family also helps protect your child from harmful content while browsing the internet. This feature uses real-time analysis of websites to detect potential threats, such as malware, phishing attempts, or inappropriate content and blocks access to those websites. It's important to note that while the Safe Browsing feature can be helpful, it's not foolproof. It's essential to discuss safe browsing habits with your child and educate them on how to recognize and avoid potential online threats.

  • Remote wipe - The Remote Wipe feature enables parents to erase the data on their child's device from a distance if it is lost or stolen. This feature helps ensure that sensitive information remains secure and your child's privacy is protected. To activate the Remote Wipe feature, you must log in to your Panda Dome account and go to the Parental Control section. Then, select the device you wish to wipe and select the option to erase the data remotely. Once it is activated, Panda Dome will delete all data stored on the chosen device, including photos, contacts, messages, and other personal information.

  • Parental dashboard - Panda Dome Family also provides a user-friendly parental dashboard that allows parents to manage all settings, view reports, and track their child's device location in one place.
