WHAT EXACTLY IS PAPAYA GLOBAL?

Papaya Global is a full-service, cloud-based payroll and workforce management platform that helps businesses manage employee compensation, tax compliance, and benefits across the globe. Designed for companies of all sizes, it offers features tailored for managing a global workforce, including support for fully remote and international teams.

The platform automates payroll processing, ensuring employees and contractors are paid on time while handling tax withholdings and filings on behalf of employers. Beyond payroll, Papaya Global provides a comprehensive suite of HR services, including benefits administration, compliance tracking, and time tracking.

Papaya Global operates on a transparent pricing model, with different plans offering varying levels of features. It integrates seamlessly with popular accounting and business tools, simplifying financial management.

Founded in 2016, Papaya Global has grown to serve businesses in over 160 countries. With its intuitive interface, automation capabilities, and strong compliance support, it eliminates the hassle of global payroll management, making it an essential tool for business owners looking to streamline operations.

WHAT ARE THE KEY FEATURES?

Papaya Global offers multiple service plans, with features varying based on the package you choose. This review will specifically focus on Papaya Global's payroll features, where the platform truly excels. Below, we'll explore the key payroll functions that make Papaya Global a standout choice for businesses.

Payroll Automation

Papaya Global streamlines payroll processing by automating key tasks, ensuring that employees and contractors are paid accurately and on time. The platform handles automatic payroll runs, calculating wages, taxes, and deductions without manual input. It also takes care of federal, state, and local tax filings, so businesses remain compliant without the hassle of paperwork. Employers can run unlimited payrolls at no extra cost, with flexible payment options including direct deposit, paper checks, or pay cards. Additionally, Papaya Global simplifies tax season by automatically generating and distributing W-2s and 1099s, reducing administrative workload and minimizing errors.

Multi-Currency Payment Options

Papaya Global offers multiple payment options, including direct deposit, prepaid debit cards, and printed checks, giving businesses flexibility in how they compensate employees. It also supports global payments, allowing companies to pay employees and contractors in local currencies. Through Papaya Global’s extensive banking network, payments are processed efficiently, avoiding unnecessary conversion fees and delays.

Tax and Compliance Management

Papaya Global ensures compliance with local tax regulations, handling payroll tax calculations, filings, and payments on your behalf. The platform automatically calculates payroll taxes, fills out the necessary tax forms, and submits them to local, state, and federal agencies, reducing compliance risks.

Employee Benefits Administration

Papaya Global makes it easy for businesses to offer employee benefits across different countries. The platform seamlessly integrates health, dental, and vision insurance, ensuring that payroll deductions are handled automatically. Employers can also provide retirement plans, savings accounts, and wellness perks, giving businesses the flexibility to offer incentives that enhance employee satisfaction.

Time Tracking & PTO Management

Papaya Global’s built-in time tracking tools simplify workforce management by allowing businesses to monitor employee hours and manage paid time off (PTO). Employees can clock in and out using a self-service portal, while the system automatically calculates overtime to ensure accurate payroll processing.

GETTING STARTED WITH PAPAYA GLOBAL

Setting up Papaya Global is a straightforward process, designed to help businesses quickly integrate payroll and workforce management into their operations. While the platform offers a comprehensive range of features, onboarding is structured to ensure a smooth transition.

Step 1: Sign Up & Choose a Plan

To begin, visit Papaya Global’s website and sign up for an account. You’ll need to select a service plan based on your business needs—whether it’s payroll, Employer of Record (EOR), or contractor management. If you're unsure which plan is best, Papaya’s team offers consultations to help determine the right fit.

Step 2: Business & Workforce Onboarding

Once registered, you’ll be guided through the onboarding process, which includes entering company details, workforce information, and compliance requirements. Papaya Global's HCM Cloud Connector allows for seamless HR data onboarding, making it easy to import employee records, even from Excel sheets.

Step 3: Compliance & Payment Setup

To ensure compliance with local tax and labor laws, you’ll need to provide relevant documentation for your workforce, including contracts, tax details, and benefits enrollment. Papaya’s team assists with compliance verification, reducing administrative burden. You’ll also set up payment methods, with options for direct deposit, digital wallets, or international bank transfers.

Step 4: Platform Training & Customization

Papaya Global offers training resources to help users navigate the platform, including self-service guides, webinars, and dedicated account managers for premium plans. You can also customize workflows, reporting dashboards, and payroll settings to match your company’s needs.

Step 5: Running Payroll & Managing Workforce

Once setup is complete, you can start running automated payroll cycles with real-time analytics and compliance monitoring. Employees and contractors gain access to the self-service portal, where they can view payslips, track benefits, and manage tax documents.

With dedicated support and automation features, Papaya Global makes global payroll and workforce management efficient, compliant, and scalable from day one.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

Papaya Global offers a range of pricing plans tailored to different workforce management needs. Here's a breakdown of their primary services and starting prices:

Payroll Plus: Designed for businesses seeking comprehensive global payroll solutions, this service is available in three tiers:

  • Grow Global: Starts at $25 per employee per month, suitable for companies with 101 to 500 employees.
  • Scale Global: Starts at $20 per employee per month, ideal for businesses with 501 to 1,000 employees.
  • Enterprise Global: Starts at $15 per employee per month, for organizations with over 1,000 employees.

    These plans offer features such as automated onboarding, payroll management, compliance support, and expense management.

    Contractor Payments & Management:: For managing and paying global contractors, this plan starts at $30 per contractor per month. It includes tools for contractor classification, management, and compliant payments.

    Employer of Record (EOR): This service enables companies to hire employees in over 160 countries without establishing a local entity. Pricing starts at $599 per employee per month, providing comprehensive support including legal, benefits, and compliance management.

    Agent of Record (AOR): For businesses looking to hire and pay contractors compliantly, this service starts at $200 per contractor per month. It offers contractor classification, management, and payment solutions.

    Global Workforce Payments: To manage and pay a global workforce in local currencies, this solution starts at $2.50 per transaction. It's ideal for businesses with extensive global payment needs.

    Please note that these are starting prices, and actual costs may vary based on specific requirements and additional services.

    FAQ

    What services does Papaya Global offer?

    Papaya Global provides a comprehensive suite of services, including global payroll management, Employer of Record (EOR) solutions, contractor management, cross-border payments, and global benefits administration. These services are designed to help businesses manage their international workforce efficiently and compliantly.

    In how many countries does Papaya Global operate?

    Papaya Global operates in over 160 countries, enabling businesses to manage payroll and workforce requirements across a vast array of regions.

    How does Papaya Global ensure compliance with local labor laws?

    Papaya Global maintains compliance by employing in-country experts who stay updated on local labor laws and regulations. Their platform automates compliance processes, ensuring that all payroll and employment practices adhere to the specific legal requirements of each country.

    What customer support does Papaya Global provide?

    Papaya Global assigns a dedicated customer success manager to each account, offering personalized support. Additionally, clients have access to in-house specialists on global employment, taxes, benefits, and labor laws, as well as a comprehensive knowledge base covering hiring practices in over 160 countries.

    Can Papaya Global integrate with existing HR systems?

    Yes, Papaya Global's platform is designed to integrate seamlessly with various HR systems, enhancing data continuity and simplifying transitions. This ensures that businesses can maintain their existing workflows while leveraging Papaya's services.

    Does Papaya Global offer an employee self-service portal?

    Yes, Papaya Global provides an employee self-service portal that allows staff to access and manage their personal information, view payslips, and handle other employment-related tasks, promoting autonomy and reducing administrative workloads.

    How does Papaya Global handle cross-border payments?

    Papaya Global's payment platform supports transactions in over 130 currencies across 160+ countries.
