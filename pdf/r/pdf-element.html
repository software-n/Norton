FEATURES

  • Edit PDFs - Get full control of your PDF documents with PDFelement's robust editing capabilities. On top of the basic editing functions, you can also edit the size, font, and color of text and images, add or remove watermarks, stamps, headers and footers, and bates numbers, or move and align objects, all without changing the original format of the document. You can even correct typing errors and spelling mistakes!

  • Convert PDFs - PDFelement lets you open and convert PDFs into 300+ formats including .doc, xls, .ppt, jpeg, gif, png, pdf/a, .log, .epub, .log, .config, RTF, and HTML, just to name a few.

  • Batch Processing - Speed things up by performing various PDF tasks in batches. You can extract data, merge files, compress, or print PDF documents in batches rather than separately.

  • Create PDFs - Create PDFs from screen captures, clipboards, and scanners or start fresh with a blank PDF.

  • Markup and Annotate - Designed with small to medium-sized businesses in mind, PDFelement offers a wide array of collaboration features. You can highlight or call attention to important parts of the document with arrows or shapes, comments, underline, and strikethrough. You can also add stamps, sticky notes, drawings, and more!

  • Create interactive and fillable PDF forms - Improve your workflow and productivity while reducing paper waste by using fillable PDF forms. Go ahead and turn existing non-fillable forms into editable forms or create a new one using pre-made templates. You can customize your forms with text fields, checkboxes, labels, radio buttons, push buttons, or signature fields.

  • Protect PDF - Control who can access or alter your PDF document by adding a password or setting restrictions on who can open, print, edit, or copy content. You can also choose to remove or hide sensitive information such as names, birth dates, addresses, Social Security numbers, account numbers, and more.

  • OCR - Forget manually scanning receipts, invoices, certificates, images, and other important documents. With PDFelement's powerful OCR feature, you can automate data capture for archiving and data collection purposes. Extract data from text, forms, images, pages, and more. Need to change anything? You can edit the document without changing the original look and format!

  • Sign PDFs - Say goodbye to wet ink signatures. PDFelement lets you fill, sign, or collect legally-binding signatures from anywhere, anytime.
