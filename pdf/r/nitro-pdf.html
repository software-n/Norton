FEATURES

  • PDF Creator - Nitro lets you create stunning and professional-looking PDFs from any device — from scratch or provided templates. No need to worry about formatting issues or missing fonts. Turning a file into an editable PDF document will not change the font, images, tables, and formatting, so it will look the same when viewed from any device!

  • PDF Editor - Do more with your PDF documents. Add corporate logos, swap images, add watermarks or dynamic bookmarks, embed clickable hyperlinks, insert page numbers, add interactive elements, and many more. You'll have everything you need to breathe life and personality into your PDF documents!

  • PDF Converter - There is no need to download any other software, do all your PDF conversion within the Nitro program. Thanks to its Nitro Cloud integration, you can even manage your PDF files from your web browser! Convert your PDF file into Word, Excel, PowerPoint, RTF, rich text, plain text, images, and many more. Need to turn it back into PDFs? Nitro can do that too. It also comes with an Outlook plugin so you can convert emails and attachments for filing. Nitro also retains the overall formatting, so you won't have to waste time fixing any inconsistencies in the post-conversion layout.

  • Merge PDFs - Combine single files into one user-friendly PDF for easy searching, organizing, sharing, printing, or reviewing. Merge different types of files, from emails to text files and spreadsheets, all with a click of a mouse! You can also do the opposite and pull separate pages from one PDF document.

  • PDF Annotator - Nitro PDF has a full-fledged annotation tool to make it easy for your team or clients to collaborate. You can add or track comments, add digital stamps, or call a collaborator's attention with callouts, shapes, or highlights. Nitro even features a measuring tool that you can use to define the distance, area, and perimeter in blueprints, CAD files, or drawings.


    • ARE THERE ANY EXTRAS?

  • OCR - Extract text from image files or convert scanned or handwritten documents into an editable and searchable PDF using Nitro's powerful OCR functionality.

  • Protect & Secure PDFs - Keep confidential information away from prying eyes with Nitro's security tools: passwords, military-grade encryption, usage permissions, digital signatures, and Chain of Trust feature.

  • Build & Fill Forms - Skip the hassle of printing, filling out, signing, and scanning documents only to re-upload them moments later. With Nitro, you can create professional-looking forms that you'll be happy to pass to your coworkers, clients, customers, and more! Make it interactive by adding different fields such as checkboxes, buttons, or multiple choices. You can then upload or request eSignatures!


    • EASE OF USE

    Installing Nitro PDF was quick and effortless. There's a quick start guide, but if you're already familiar with MS Office's ribbon-style UX, you can go ahead and skip it. Nitro is fully integrated into the cloud, so you can pull your files anywhere or collaborate with international team members. It also integrates with other cloud services such as Google Drive, OneDrive, or Xbox to further optimize your workflow routine. All in all, we find Nitro PDF easy and reliable.


    PRICING AND PLANS

    You can try Nitro's full functionality with a 14-day trial. After that period, Nitro PDF Pro goes for USD143.99 per user for a one-time license. If you're buying for more than one user, it is best to take advantage of their package discounts.


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    During your trial period, you will only have access to the Knowledgebase section, Community Forum, and License Portal. There's no phone or chat support, but everything is pretty intuitive, so it's unlikely you'll ever need help from customer support anyway. In any case, once you've purchased a license, you can open a support ticket for direct assistance.
