WHAT IS TURBOTAX?

TurboTax, developed by Intuit, has been a powerhouse in the tax preparation software industry for decades and continues to set the standard. It expertly balances substance and style, offering a seamless tax-filing experience with a user-friendly, interview-style format. The software asks simple questions about your financial situation and automatically fills out the appropriate tax forms based on your responses, ensuring accuracy and efficiency.

TurboTax covers a wide range of tax scenarios, from basic W-2 income to more complex returns involving investments, rental properties, and self-employment income. It is updated annually to reflect the latest IRS regulations, so you can be confident that your return is compliant. Recent enhancements, such as integrations with Credit Karma and QuickBooks Online, a more intuitive interview process, and a streamlined wrap-up, make TurboTax even more user-friendly. Its top-rated mobile apps allow for smooth, on-the-go tax filing.

While TurboTax comes at a premium price, its depth of tax coverage, automation, and first-class support justify the cost. It is always top on our list and is a frequent top-ranking choice for its ability to simplify even the most complex tax situations.


WHO SHOULD USE TURBOTAX?

TurboTax is an excellent choice for a wide range of tax filers, especially students and individuals with simple tax returns who qualify for TurboTax Free Edition. In fact, 37% of taxpayers meet the criteria for this free option, which supports Form 1040 returns with no additional schedules, except for the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC), Child Tax Credit (CTC), and Student Loan Interest Deduction.

If your tax situation is more complex, TurboTax still provides a seamless filing experience, but it comes at a higher cost. Want to claim housing deductions or itemize charitable donations? You’ll need to upgrade to the Deluxe tier. If you earn rental income or sell stocks, you’ll be required to move up to the Premium tier. While these upgrades can be pricey, many users find TurboTax’s industry-leading software, automation, and ease of use worth the cost—especially compared to manually entering tax information on other platforms.

However, there are more affordable options for certain tax situations. Since TurboTax charges $39 per state return, those who lived in one state but worked in another in 2024 may find a flat-fee alternative, like Jackson Hewitt’s $25 unlimited state returns option, more cost-effective. Similarly, self-employed individuals might save money by using TaxSlayer, which offers competitive pricing for business deductions and freelancer tax filings.


WHAT ARE THE FEATURES?

Below are some of the key features that make TurboTax one of the most trusted and widely used tax preparation software solutions available today:

Guided Tax Preparation: TurboTax simplifies the tax filing process with its interview-style approach, guiding you step by step through a series of easy-to-understand questions about your income, deductions, and credits. Instead of manually entering data into complex tax forms, you’ll answer straightforward prompts, and TurboTax will fill in the appropriate sections automatically. The software also provides real-time explanations to help you understand why certain entries impact your return. This method reduces errors, minimizes confusion, and ensures that you file accurately and efficiently, even if you’re unfamiliar with tax laws.

Integration with Financial Tools: TurboTax integrates seamlessly with QuickBooks, Credit Karma, and other financial tools, making it easy to import income, expenses, and deductions directly from your records. If you’re self-employed or own a small business, this feature is especially valuable, as it eliminates the need for manual data entry, reducing errors and ensuring you don’t miss deductible expenses. TurboTax also allows you to upload W-2s and 1099 forms by simply taking a photo, automatically pulling the relevant information into your tax return.

Access to Tax Experts: For users who want professional guidance, TurboTax Live Assisted and Full Service plans connect you with certified tax professionals, CPAs, and EAs (Enrolled Agents). These experts can answer questions in real-time, review your return before you file, or even handle the entire process for you. Whether you need help with complex deductions, self-employment taxes, or IRS notices, having access to tax professionals ensures peace of mind. TurboTax also offers an Audit Support Guarantee, providing guidance if you’re ever audited by the IRS.

Mobile Accessibility: The TurboTax mobile app brings the full power of the software to your smartphone or tablet, allowing you to file taxes from anywhere. With the app, you can upload tax documents by snapping a photo, track the status of your refund in real time, and receive notifications about any necessary actions. The app is designed to provide the same seamless experience as the desktop version, making it ideal for busy professionals, frequent travelers, or anyone who prefers mobile convenience.

Maximum Refund Guarantee: TurboTax guarantees that you’ll receive the maximum refund you’re entitled to. The software scans for every available deduction and credit, ensuring you don’t leave money on the table. If you find a larger refund or owe less using another tax preparation method, TurboTax will refund the applicable purchase price of the software. This guarantee reinforces TurboTax’s commitment to accuracy and tax optimization.

Comprehensive Coverage: TurboTax handles a wide range of tax situations, making it suitable for individuals, families, investors, freelancers, and small business owners. It supports deductions for education, medical expenses, mortgage interest, charitable contributions, and student loans, as well as income sources like rental properties, stock investments, gig work, and self-employment earnings. The software is updated annually to reflect changes in tax laws, ensuring that you file with the most current IRS regulations and state tax requirements. Whether your tax situation is simple or complex, TurboTax provides the coverage and guidance you need.


IS IT EASY TO USE?

Yes, TurboTax is designed with user experience in mind. Its intuitive interface guides you through each step of the tax filing process, breaking down complex tax concepts into understandable terms. The software provides explanations and tips throughout, helping you make informed decisions. Additionally, the ability to import financial data and previous tax returns streamlines the process, saving you time and reducing the likelihood of errors.


USER INTERFACE

TurboTax offers a user-friendly interface that simplifies the tax filing process, even for those without prior experience. The platform employs an interview-style approach, asking straightforward questions about your financial situation and personal circumstances. This method ensures that you only address sections pertinent to your tax scenario, minimizing confusion and unnecessary data entry.

The dashboard provides a clear, real-time overview of your progress, allowing you to navigate seamlessly between different sections. Visual aids, such as infographics and summaries, break down complex information, making it more digestible and less intimidating. For instance, as you input data, TurboTax updates your estimated refund or tax due amount, offering immediate feedback on how each entry impacts your overall tax picture.

Moreover, TurboTax integrates with various financial tools, enabling the import of financial data directly from banks and employers. This feature reduces manual data entry and potential errors, streamlining the entire process. The software also includes automated error-checking mechanisms, flagging inconsistencies or missing information to ensure accuracy before submission.


DESKTOP AND MOBILE APPS

TurboTax gives you the flexibility to file your taxes on the platform that best suits your needs, whether you prefer working on a desktop or mobile device. If you like filing from your computer, the desktop version provides the same comprehensive features as the online version, with the added benefit of local file storage for extra security and offline access. This is especially useful if you want to keep full control over your financial data or work without needing an internet connection.

If you prefer filing on the go, TurboTax’s mobile app for iOS and Android makes the process seamless. You can start your return on one device and pick up where you left off on another, giving you complete flexibility. The app allows you to upload tax documents by simply snapping a photo, automatically extracting key information to reduce manual data entry. You’ll also get real-time access to refund tracking, tax updates, and support resources, making it easy to stay on top of your taxes wherever you are.

Whether you prefer the detailed control of desktop filing or the convenience of mobile access, TurboTax ensures a smooth, efficient, and user-friendly experience across all platforms.


IS IT SAFE AND SECURE?

Yes, TurboTax is designed with multiple layers of security to protect your personal and financial information. Intuit employs industry-leading security practices and advanced technology, continuously improving its cybersecurity measures in collaboration with security researchers worldwide. TurboTax uses TLS encryption to secure data in transit and AES-256 encryption to protect stored information, preventing unauthorized access.

To further enhance account security, TurboTax requires multi-factor authentication (MFA), adding an extra layer of protection beyond just a password. It also offers biometric authentication options, such as fingerprint and facial recognition, making it easier and more secure to access your account from mobile devices. Additionally, TurboTax employs real-time monitoring and fraud detection tools to identify and prevent unauthorized activity, sending immediate alerts if suspicious behavior is detected.

TurboTax also ensures automatic backups and secure cloud storage, so your tax returns remain safe and easily retrievable, even in the event of accidental deletion or system failures. As an IRS-approved tax software, TurboTax complies with federal and state regulations, meeting stringent security and privacy standards.

While TurboTax provides robust security features, you should always take additional precautions when entering sensitive information online. Use a trusted network, such as your home Wi-Fi, rather than public or unsecured connections. Enabling strong, unique passwords and activating MFA on your TurboTax account can further enhance your security. By combining Intuit’s top-tier safeguards with smart personal security habits, you can ensure your sensitive tax data remains well-protected.


IS THERE A FREE VERSION?

Yes, TurboTax offers a Free Edition for simple tax returns, making it a great option for individuals with basic filing needs. This free version supports Form 1040 and allows you to claim the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC), Child Tax Credit (CTC), and Student Loan Interest Deduction. It’s ideal for W-2 employees with no additional income sources, investments, or itemized deductions.

However, if your tax situation is more complex—such as needing to report freelance income, rental properties, stock sales, or itemized deductions—you’ll be required to upgrade to a paid plan. Additionally, while the Free Edition covers federal tax filing, state returns may come with an additional charge, depending on your location.

TurboTax frequently updates its free filing criteria, so it's always worth checking whether your specific tax situation qualifies. If you need extra guidance, TurboTax also offers Live Assisted Basic for simple returns, which provides expert support but may require an upgrade after the initial promotional period.


HOW MUCH IS IT?

TurboTax offers a variety of plans tailored to different tax situations, allowing you to choose the level of assistance and features that best suit your needs. Below is an overview of the available plans:

1. Free Edition

  • Cost: $0 for federal and state returns

  • Ideal for: Simple tax situations, such as W-2 income, limited interest and dividend income, and claiming standard deductions.

  • Features: Guided preparation for Form 1040, ability to claim Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC), Child Tax Credit (CTC), and student loan interest deduction.


    • 2. Deluxe

  • Cost: Starting at $59 for federal returns; additional fees for state returns

  • Ideal for: Taxpayers looking to maximize deductions and credits, including homeowners and those with medical expenses or charitable contributions.

  • Features: Everything in Free Edition, plus the ability to itemize deductions, access to over 350 tax deductions and credits, and one-on-one support from a tax specialist.


    • 3. Premier

  • Cost: $0 for federal and state returns

  • Ideal for: Simple tax situations, such as W-2 income, limited interest and dividend income, and claiming standard deductions.

  • Features: Guided preparation for Form 1040, ability to claim Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC), Child Tax Credit (CTC), and student loan interest deduction.


    • 4. Self-Employed

  • Cost: Starting at $119 for federal returns; additional fees for state returns

  • Ideal for: Freelancers, contractors, and small business owners.

  • Features: Includes Premier features, plus assistance in finding industry-specific deductions, expense tracking, and integration with QuickBooks Self-Employed.


    • 5. TurboTax Live Assisted

  • Cost: Starting at $89 for federal returns; additional fees for state returns

  • Ideal for: Taxpayers seeking on-demand assistance from tax experts.

  • Features: Access to live tax advice, a final review of your return by a tax expert, and all features of the corresponding DIY package (Deluxe, Premier, or Self-Employed).


    • 6. TurboTax Live Full Service

  • Cost: Starting at $209 for federal returns; additional fees for state returns

  • Ideal for: Individuals who prefer to have a dedicated tax expert handle the entire filing process.

  • Features: A tax expert prepares, signs, and files your return for you, with year-round audit support and advice.


    • Additional Considerations:

  • State Returns: State tax returns are not included in the federal pricing and typically incur additional fees.

  • Promotions and Discounts: TurboTax occasionally offers discounts, especially for early filers or through affiliate partnerships.

  • Military Members: Active-duty military personnel may qualify for free or discounted services.


    • CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    Our experience with TurboTax’s customer service has been largely positive, with a range of support options available to assist users throughout the tax filing process. TurboTax offers multiple ways to get help, including phone support, live chat, email assistance, and a robust self-help knowledge base. The TurboTax Digital Assistant provides instant answers to common questions, while the built-in guidance tools offer explanations and recommendations as you fill out your return.

    One of the standout features is TurboTax Live Assisted, which connects you with a certified tax expert, CPA, or EA in real-time. This service has been highly praised by users who appreciate having expert guidance at their fingertips. For those who want a completely hands-off experience, TurboTax Live Full Service allows a tax professional to handle the entire filing process for you.

    The TurboTax Community Forum is another valuable resource, where users can ask questions and receive responses from both tax professionals and experienced filers. Many users find that they can quickly resolve their concerns through the self-service options without needing to contact customer support directly.

    While peak tax season can sometimes lead to longer wait times, TurboTax’s priority support for paid plans ensures that users receive timely and efficient assistance. Overall, our experience with TurboTax’s customer service has been smooth and reliable, making it easy to get help when needed.


    BOTTOMLINE

    TurboTax offers a comprehensive and user-friendly tax filing experience, catering to a wide range of financial situations—whether you’re single, married, a homeowner, or self-employed. Each plan is packed with features designed to maximize efficiency and accuracy, from seamless data imports to real-time refund explanations. Helpful tax calculators, tips, and integrations with tools like QuickBooks make the process even smoother, ensuring transparency and ease of use.

    While TurboTax comes at a higher price than some competitors, its automation, expert support, and refund-maximization tools can make it worth the investment. If you prefer a simpler, lower-cost option and don’t mind manually entering tax data, a budget-friendly alternative might be a better fit. But for those looking for a stress-free, feature-rich tax filing solution, TurboTax remains one of the best choices available.
