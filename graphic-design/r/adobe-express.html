Adobe Express Review

9.0
Very Good

SHORT ON TIME? HERE’S A QUICK SUMMARY

Adobe Express is a great photo editing tool for beginners and professionals alike. Serving as a cheaper alternative to Photoshop, Adobe Express is perfect for those who need to quickly edit and share photos without all of the bells and whistles. You might have known Adobe Express by its former name, Adobe Spark.

👍 PROS:
  • Cross-device and cross-platform compatibility
  • Powerful photo editing capabilities
  • Dozens of built-in filters and effects
  • Easy photo sharing
  • Affordable
👎 CONS:
  • Lack of some advanced features found in Photoshop
  • Power-hungry
  • Some tools can be challenging to use at first

FEATURES

As part of the Adobe Creative Cloud, Express has a reputation to maintain. It is part of a family of powerful tools used by creatives all over the world. Despite being a less comprehensive tool than Photoshop, Adobe Express still packs a punch when it comes to photo editing.

As Adobe's response to competitors like Canva, Adobe Express provides designers with an easy way to create stunning graphics, photos, and videos all in one place. With the Express app, you can access your files from any device, making it an excellent option for those who need to work on the go.

Here are some of its standout features:

  • Faster designs with Adobe Stock + Templates - First, Adobe Express integrates with Adobe Stock. This gives you access to over 100 million high-quality photos, videos, illustrations, and templates, all within the app. That saves you time from having to search for free stock photos online.

    Second, Adobe Express has a library of customizable templates. You can create professional-looking designs without having to start from scratch. By combining these two features, Adobe Express provides everything you need to design stunning photos and graphics quickly.

  • Edit photos like a pro - With Adobe Express, you can easily edit and enhance your images. The app provides you with all the essential photo editing tools, such as crop, rotate, color correction, and exposure adjustment. In addition, there are dozens of built-in filters and effects that you can use to give your photos a unique look.

    The best part about the photo editing tools in Adobe Express is that they are non-destructive. That means that you can always revert back to the original photo if you don't like the changes you've made.

  • Start for free, then pay for what you need - Adobe Express is free to download and use. However, there are some premium features that you can optionally subscribe to. You clearly see the distinction between free and premium features within the app. Icons, fonts, and graphics are clearly labeled as such.

    Once you've reached a plateau with the free features, you can subscribe to premium, keeping everything you've already designed. You can also cancel anytime, so you're never locked in.

  • Automatic resizing - Since most people share their photos online, Adobe Express automatically optimizes your photos for different social media platforms. You don't have to worry about manually resizing your photos before sharing them.

    For example, Instagram photos are typically square, so Adobe Express will automatically crop your images to fit that format. You can also choose to share your photos in their original aspect ratio if you prefer. When you start the sharing process, Adobe Express will show you a preview of how your photo will look on each platform.

  • Work on multiple devices - You can use Adobe Express on numerous devices with a single Adobe Creative Cloud account. All your projects and photos are synced across devices, so you can pick up where you left off no matter where you are. Compatibility extends to your computer, tablet, and smartphone.

    In addition, Adobe Express automatically saves your changes as you work. You never have to worry about losing your work if something happens to your device.

  • Download options - Adobe Express allows you to download your photos in JPEG, PDF, or PNG format. No need to run a separate program to convert your file types.


    • PRICING AND PLANS

    As mentioned above, Adobe Express is free to download and use. However, functionality will be limited unless you upgrade to a paid plan.

    There are two paid plan options: Adobe Creative Cloud Single App and Adobe Creative Cloud All Apps. The former gives you access to all the features in Adobe Express and costs $9.99/month or $99.99/year. There is a 30-day free trial available.

    The latter includes access to all the features in Adobe Express and over 20 other Adobe Creative Cloud apps like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro. It costs $54.99/month, with a 7-day free trial available.

    When you sign up for Express premium, you get access to extra features like:

    • Scheduled posting
    • 20,000 Adobe fonts
    • 160 million high-quality stock images
    • Branding and logo tools
    • 100 GB of storage
    • And more!


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    Finally, no matter which plans you choose, you'll have access to customer support. Adobe is known for its excellent customer service, so you can rest assured that they'll take care of you. They have a robust customer support system, with options like live chat, phone support, and email support.
    And if you don't want to contact customer support just yet, Adobe also has an extensive help center with FAQs, tutorials, and more. Plus, since many people use the program for its popularity, there's a good chance you can find the answer to your question just by doing a quick Google search. The community forum is also a great place to ask questions and get advice from other Adobe Express users.
