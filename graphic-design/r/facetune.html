WHAT IS FACETUNE?

Facetune, developed by Lightricks, is a popular photo-editing app focused on enhancing selfies and portraits on mobile devices. It offers various editing tools for tasks like smoothing skin, whitening teeth, removing blemishes, and reshaping facial features. Lightricks, an Israeli software development company, gained significant attention when Facetune went viral in 2015, becoming the fifth best-selling paid iOS app and revolutionizing the way people edit and share photos on social media.


PHOTO EDITING FEATURES

By 2024, Facetune has garnered over 200 million downloads, making it a go-to choice for photo editing. Apart from the fundamental tools like Object and Background Remover, Photo Filter & Effects, Blur, and Selfie & Avatar Generator, Facetune stands out from its competitors by offering the following exciting features:

Hair Color & Style

Facetune offers a hair color and style try-on feature that lets you experiment with virtual hairstyles and colors effortlessly. With just a tap, you can switch between various styles and shades, allowing you to explore different looks without any commitment. Whether you're craving bold blowouts, soft curls, or layered cuts, Facetune's Hairstyles Try-On lets you visualize them directly from your phone, eliminating any post-hairdresser regrets.

Photo Retouch

Facetune's Photo Retouch feature offers quick and easy photo editing tools powered by AI, allowing you to enhance your selfies, portraits, and images in seconds. With just a tap, you can achieve instant makeovers.

The Face Editor provides various AI-powered tools for retouching:

  • Heal: Spot-treat blemishes in seconds with AI photo retouching, ensuring flawless skin.

  • Smooth: Even out your skin texture effortlessly.

  • Texture: Add realistic textures to your skin after smoothing for a natural appearance.

  • Conceal: Easily conceal under-eye circles for a refreshed look, perfect for those late-night selfies.

  • Additionally, you can whiten teeth in photos with a simple swipe. Pro tip: You can also use the teeth whitening tool on other objects like sneakers!

    • Makeup Photo Editor

    Facetune's Makeup feature offers a virtual makeup try-on tool that allows you to experiment with different makeup looks without any mess. You can easily add makeup to your photos, whether you want to keep your makeup looking fresh or try out new trends.

  • Full Glam in Seconds: Choose from a variety of digital makeup presets in the Beauty Looks feed to add a complete chic makeup look to your photos with just a tap.

  • One-Tap Makeup Editor: Select a beauty look preset, adjust the intensity, and transform your look from everyday to runway without any hassle.

  • Fine-Tune the Filter: Adjust the intensity of existing beauty filters to perfectly enhance your photo's overall aesthetic, including aspects like lighting and tone.

  • Photo Enhancer: Brighten, smooth, and enhance your entire photo with the AI image enhancer for a glowy finished look.

  • Photo Cropper: Quickly crop images to the desired size for sharing on platforms like Instagram, directly from the Beauty Looks feed.

  • Change Backgrounds: Easily change the background of your photo with automatic selection and adjustment for enhanced appeal.

  • Manual Control: For detailed edits, head to the full photo editor to tweak each aspect of your look with tools like water-resistant eyeliner, smudge-proof lipstick, and more.

  • Experiment with Lipstick and Blush Shades: Use the Paint tool to adjust and add tones to your cheeks and lips, ensuring your makeup is exactly as you envision it down to the smallest detail.

    • AI Outfit Changer

    Facetune's AI Outfit Changer revolutionizes your wardrobe by allowing you to effortlessly edit clothes in photos. With this AI-powered tool, you can try on a new outfit every day, exploring trendy shirts, chic dresses, and fun colors with just a tap.

    Create your dream digital closet with the virtual try-on clothes feature, experimenting with the latest fashion trends without the need to buy or try on physical garments. Customize your outfits further by changing colors, textures, and patterns, whether you're exploring fabrics like lace and leather or opting for denim blues and casual prints.


    FACETUNE 2.0 NEW FEATURES

    Facetune 2's strength lies in its advanced artificial intelligence (AI) and augmented reality (AR), particularly its face-recognition-driven 3D modeling. While other photo apps like Snapchat and Snapseed also utilize 3D-modeling face recognition, Facetune's implementation stands out as the most impressive on a mobile app. Here are some of the standout features introduced in the latest update:

  • Face Reshaping: This feature gives you the power to subtly adjust the contours and proportions of your face in photos. With this tool, you can refine facial features like your jawline, cheekbones, nose, and chin to get the look you want. Whether you're aiming to slim down your face, enhance your cheekbones, or make other tweaks, Face Reshaping offers intuitive controls for precise editing. This feature lets you enhance your portraits while keeping a natural appearance.

  • Intelligent shooting: Facetune's Intelligent Shooting feature takes your selfie game to the next level. With this feature, you can shoot selfies directly within the app and utilize its artificial intelligence capabilities to adjust slider settings and preview results before capturing your shot. As you aim your camera at yourself, an augmented reality live image editing feature kicks in, enabling you to smooth your skin, whiten your teeth, resize your nose, and enhance your eyes and lips—all in real-time and through the lens. This innovative technology relies on high-fidelity 3D facial modeling to seamlessly integrate these improvements with your natural features, ensuring a flawless result every time.

  • Relight: Facetune's Relight feature offers impressive control over lighting in your portraits. With a simple swipe, you can adjust the light source, effectively changing the direction of light and shadow. This feature utilizes face recognition and AI to create a 3D model of the image, allowing you to manipulate the lighting for a desired effect. Whether you want to remove under-eye circles or brighten your entire portrait, Relight provides a powerful solution for enhancing photos taken in suboptimal lighting conditions.


    • EASE OF USE

    Facetune offers a user-friendly experience, making photo editing simple and accessible. Its intuitive platform presents popular filter and effect options directly on the homepage, ensuring easy navigation. You can explore special filters curated for you or browse through all effects until you find the perfect one—all it takes is a tap to select the desired filter and upload your photo for editing. With AI handling the editing process, you receive a polished result in seconds.


    PERFORMANCE

    Facetune impresses with its performance across the board.

    Startup Time: Launching Facetune is a breeze, taking just a few seconds with minimal loading screens. Once you tap the app, it springs to life almost as swiftly as the camera app on your phone.

    Lag & Stability: Facetune operates seamlessly, offering a smooth user experience. Filters and effects usually download in a matter of seconds, depending on your internet speed. Whether it's a filter, editing preset, or AI-powered effect, everything is ready for use without delay.

    AI Editing & Integrations: The AI works its magic in mere seconds, allowing you to fine-tune your photos afterward. You can instantly share edited photos on various social media platforms like Instagram and TikTok or send them to friends via WhatsApp, Messenger, or iMessage.


    OUTPUT QUALITY

    In Facetune, the quality of edited photos typically remains high, as the app is engineered to retain the original integrity of your images. Whether you're smoothing skin, whitening teeth, or reshaping facial features, Facetune applies edits while striving to preserve clarity and overall image quality. Utilizing advanced algorithms, the app ensures natural-looking edits without compromising image integrity, offering fine-tuning options for users to adjust edit intensity while maintaining original details. However, final photo quality may also depend on factors like original image resolution, extent of edits, and user proficiency.


    INTEGRATION

    Facetune seamlessly integrates with popular photo-sharing apps. You can instantly share photos with friends via iMessage, Messenger, WhatsApp, and more directly within the Facetune platform. It also connects with Instagram and Facebook, allowing you to share AI-edited photos directly to your feed. This makes posting and sharing stunning photos quicker and easier, especially for busy influencers.


    PRIVACY AND SECURITY

    Facetune takes your privacy and security seriously, with measures in place to protect your photos and personal information. The company has a privacy policy that outlines how they collect, use, and safeguard your data.

    When it comes to storing and using photos, Facetune does not sell your images. You can edit your photos directly on your device, and the edited images are usually stored locally on your device. While Facetune may collect anonymous usage data for analytics, this information is not personally identifiable and is solely used to enhance the app's performance and improve your experience.


    PRICING AND PLANS

    Facetune offers both free and paid options for users. Even with the free version, you can access AI features like makeup, hair color and style, and outfit changer, typically paid features in similar apps. If you want to try out the premium editing tools, Facetune provides a 7-day trial. After that, you'll need to subscribe for unlimited access to advanced editing tools. The subscription model offers a choice between a monthly plan for $9.99/month, an annual plan for $59.99/year, or a one-time lifetime purchase for $199.99.


    THE VERDICT

    Facetune makes it easy to enhance your appearance, providing a user-friendly experience for enjoyable portrait editing. Just be careful not to go overboard and change your natural look. It's a great confidence booster for social media and family photos. The app runs smoothly, allowing effortless adjustments and providing multiple undo options for quick fixes. While the live preview feature can be fun to experiment with, you might notice that resulting images appear slightly soft. If you prefer, you might find more satisfaction in editing existing photos. I suggest starting with the free version, especially if you're already familiar with the previous version. Then, if you feel the need for more features, you can consider upgrading.
