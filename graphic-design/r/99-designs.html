WHAT IS 99DESIGNS?

99designs is an online marketplace that connects businesses with freelance designers. The platform is designed to serve as a digital graphic design company that you can rely on to find high-quality designs that meet your needs.

Wondering what kind of designs you can find on 99designs? You’ll find almost anything that is design-related. This platform has a wide range of design services for you to explore, such as:

  • Logo design

  • Web design

  • Social media page design

  • Merchandise design

  • Packaging design

  • Book cover design

    • And so much more! The wide range of available designs is made possible by thousands of skilled designers from all over the world who are ready to create the perfect design for you. In this community, you're sure to find top talent to help you in any design area.


    HOW DOES 99DESIGN WORK?

    Before you can start browsing through the many designs available on this platform, you need to understand how the platform works. Luckily, the process isn't complicated as it involves only three major steps. These are:

    1. Creating a Simple Design Brief

      2. A design brief is simply a document containing all the instructions and guidelines for your desired design. The designers will use this document to create designs that meet your specific requirements.

      You're probably worried that creating a brief will be too complicated and time-consuming. However, 99designs has a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to create a brief for your project. A wizard will guide you through the process of creating a brief step-by-step.

      You will start by selecting your design category. A wizard will give you some samples, so you select the one that matches your interests. You’ll also need to provide more details about your design and select a color scheme.

    2. Connecting With a Designer

      3. This is where you find the perfect designer for your project. You can either request 1-to-1 projects or launch a design contest.

      This is where you find the perfect designer for your project. You can either request 1-to-1 projects or launch a design contest.

      You can launch a design contest if you want to give other designers a chance to work on your project. In this case, all designers on the platform will be allowed to submit their designs for your project.

      After launching the contest, you will start receiving designs from the designers. You can provide feedback to help the designers create designs that match your expectations.

      You can also request a 99designs pro consultation service to help you launch your contest and receive high-quality designs. 99designs will assign you a representative who can match you with a designer who meets your needs.

    3. Collaborating With a Designer

    After finding the perfect designer for your project, it's time to start collaborating. Remember, effective communication is key to ensuring a successful project.

    Fortunately, 99designs has a messaging system that makes it easy for you and your designer to communicate. You can use this messaging system to provide feedback, ask questions, and give suggestions.

    Once the design satisfies you, you can go ahead and approve it and release payment. The designer will then send you the final design files, which you can use for your project.
