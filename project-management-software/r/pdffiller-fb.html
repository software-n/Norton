    IS IT SAFE AND SECURE?

    Yes, PDFfiller is a safe and secure platform for managing digital forms and documents. It uses enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and compliance with major regulatory standards.

    PDFfiller is GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 compliant, ensuring that businesses handling sensitive or confidential information—such as legal contracts, medical records, or financial documents—can trust that their data is securely stored and managed. The platform also includes role-based access controls (RBAC), allowing you to set specific permissions for users, ensuring that only authorized individuals can view, edit, or share certain documents.
