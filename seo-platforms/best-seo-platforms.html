    What is SEO?


    Semrush defines SEO as "the art and science of persuading search engines such as Google, Bing, and Yahoo, to recommend your content to their users as the best solution to their problem." It stands for Search Engine Optimization and refers to the process of increasing your site's visibility in organic (not paid) search engine results.

    Why is it important to rank on search engines?


    Unless you spend a lot of money advertising on multiple platforms, your site won't get much traffic without SEO. A good SEO strategy will increase your site's ranking and make it more visible to potential customers. Ranking high is important because studies show that 75% of internet users never scroll past the first page of search results.

    So let's say that you're selling specialty coffee beans in Denver. When someone types "best coffee beans denver" into Google, you'd want them to stumble onto your site. For that to happen, you need to rank above all the other similar websites. That's what SEO is all about.

    Why should I pay for an SEO tool?


    Performing comprehensive SEO audits regularly is key to maintaining a healthy website. It can help flag up website issues like slow site speed or site map errors, show you what your competitors are doing and ranking for, or improve your conversion rates.

    Unfortunately, generating a thorough SEO audit by hand requires a lot of time and military-grade organizational skills. Can you imagine if you're handling multiple websites? When all the keyword research and data analysis becomes overwhelming, it is recommended to get a decent SEO tool to automate the process.

    Of course, you can always hire an SEO agency, but that will cost a lot more money, and the results tend to plateau over time. Remember, SEO is a never-ending game, so you would need their services for as long as your website is up. This is why it's worth investing in a good SEO software. These tools can cut the cost and time spent on SEO in half, while delivering the same (or better) results.

    What features should I look for in an SEO platform?


    With so many options on the market, choosing the best SEO tool can be a time-consuming task. For you to make an informed decision, here's a checklist of features that a good SEO software should offer:

    • Keyword research tools
    • Website monitoring tools
    • Unrestricted access to the data in one place
    • Competitor analysis tools
    • Repeatable workflows
    • Site audits with unlimited crawls
    • Link management tools
    • Actionable AI-driven insights
    • Platform adoption and live chat
    • AI that remains one-step ahead

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


    📊 What are SEO platforms?

    SEO platforms are software systems that bring together many of the tools needed to optimize or improve site ranking. These tools address SEO functionalities such as keyword research, competitor analysis, rank tracking, content marketing analytics, link building, on-page SEO, and many more.

    📅 How long does SEO take?

    It usually takes between 4 to 12 months to start seeing results from your SEO efforts. Bear in mind that several factors can impact this timeframe, such as your website's history, competition, and resources.

    📉 Why don't I advertise and skip SEO altogether?

    SEO and PPC (Pay-Per-Click) are not mutually exclusive. These two traffic-generating approaches complement each other and give better results when combined. However, there are several advantages to using SEO. Customers usually trust websites that show up at the top of the organic search results because they know they are not advertisements. Organic listings also typically have better click-through rates, longevity, and better reach than paid search ads.

    💵 What is the best free SEO tool?

    There are tons of free SEO tools out there, but their features are always limited compared to the paid versions. However, if you just want to do some keyword research or stay on top of your analytics, you can check out the free versions of the software recommended in our list. You can always upgrade to the paid version later if you feel the extra tools behind the paywall are necessary for what you need to rank higher on google and other search engines.
