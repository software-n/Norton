WILL IT KEEP ME SAFE AND ANONYMOUS ONLINE?

NordVPN was founded in 2012 and is currently headquartered in Panama. Great news for the privacy-conscious because this country has no mandatory retention laws. It is also not a member of intelligence alliances like Five Eyes or Fourteen Eyes. What does this all mean for you? It means NordVPN is not required to store logs, so you won't have to worry about the government agencies getting their hands on your carbon footprint - your IP address, online activities, browsing history, and more. True enough, when we checked NordVPN's privacy policy page, we found the following excerpt:

"Being based in Panama allows us to keep no logs of users' online activity (no IP addresses, browsing data, traffic information, etc.). This means that we are unable to link an individual user to a specific IP address, connection timestamp or other information that we do not collect. Therefore, even in cases we receive a rightfully served request, it might be impossible for us to identify a specific person or provide any identifying information related to that person."

When it comes to security, few other VPNs can match what NordVPN has to offer. There's more to it than the usual 256-bit AES encryption, automatic kill switch, and split-tunneling features. NordVPN offers multi-layered protection with a host of advanced security features, starting with a double VPN that encrypts your traffic twice. This is an excellent feature targeted towards journalists or political activists, or just anyone who wants to avoid surveillance by the authorities.

There's also Onion Over VPN, which combines the anonymity of Tor with the security of a VPN tunnel. The Reserved IP Address feature is also a nice addition. Similar to dedicated IP addresses, this simply means that your assigned IP address won't be shared with anyone else. This means you won't have to bother with CAPTCHA checks to prove that you're an actual person and not a bot. It makes online payments easy and reduces the risks of your IP address getting blacklisted from some websites.

You can also turn on CyberSec to block ads and malicious websites and prevent malware attacks, phishing scams, and botnet control. And then there's NordPass - a sleek password manager that lets you store unlimited passwords, keep credit card details, and share notes such as WiFi passwords securely. Finally, there's NordLocker. It's a highly secure cloud storage platform that secures your files in a single click and backs them up on a private cloud. Neat, but you only get 3GB of cloud storage for free. If you need more, and you most certainly will, you can upgrade to 500GB for $3.99 per month.


HOW FAST IS IT?

So NordVPN is safe and secure, but is it fast? We set out to determine if this VPN is fast enough for gaming, video conferencing, and other bandwidth-heavy activities. Our base speed before connecting to NordVPN was at 250 Mbps. We then tried a local server in New York, and our speed dipped at 203Mbps. No surprise there, the real test is once we connect to a server located overseas. We then chose a server in Singapore and recorded a speed loss of 51%. With a download speed of 122Mbps, we hardly noticed any impact on all activities, and one of our staff even managed to win 2 matches in Battle Royale without missing any shots. We tried a few other servers using NordVPNs auto connect feature, and in all instances, our download speed never went below 100Mbps. Out of all the VPNs we’ve tested, NordVPN has been one of the most stable on both local and distant servers.


PRICING AND PLANS

At first glance, NordVPN seems to be on the pricey side, with their month-to-month plan setting you back $11.95 per month. Their annual plan sounds more reasonable at $4.92 per month, but still not exactly the cheapest. However, commit to a 2-year plan, and you see this price go down to only $3.67 per month (+3 months free). Come to think of it, if you decide to split the bill with a friend, it's practically pocket change. All of their plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee, so you can try it and cancel at any time if you're not entirely happy.


CUSTOMER SUPPORT

NordVPN's knowledge base is conveniently divided into 4 categories: FAQ, general info, billing, and connectivity. If you don't feel like digging around for answers, you can always get through support via their live chat feature. It's open 24/7, and we tried it during on and off-peak hours. Both times, the experience had been pleasant. We were initially greeted by a chatbot that presented us with a bunch of keywords. Just reply with "live agent", and it'll send you over to a live chat representative. If you'd rather send an email, you can send them a message at support@nordvpn.com.


THE VERDICT

As you can see from our comprehensive review, NordVPN ticks all the right boxes - ease of use, speed, security, privacy, apps for every device, and 24/7 customer support. Considering all the extras like CyberSec, NordPass, and dedicated servers, NordVPN felt like a bargain compared to other VPNs in its price range.
