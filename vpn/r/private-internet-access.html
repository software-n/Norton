WHO IS BEHIND PRIVATE INTERNET ACCESS?

Private Internet Access (PIA) was founded in 2010 by London Trust Media, Inc. as a VPN service provider. The company was started to provide privacy and security solutions to Internet users and to promote Internet freedom. In 2019, it was acquired by Kape Technologies, which also owns two other reputable VPNs: Cyberghost and ExpressVPN.

Kape Technologies is a cybersecurity and online privacy company headquartered in Tel Aviv, Israel. It was formed in 2016, and provides a range of internet security products and services, including anti-malware software, virtual private networks (VPNs), and web security solutions.

The acquisition was part of Kape's strategy to expand its portfolio of cybersecurity and online privacy products. PIA is a well-known VPN provider, known for its strong privacy policies and user-friendly software. The acquisition of PIA helped Kape to strengthen its position in the VPN market and provide a comprehensive range of privacy and security solutions to its customers.

IS PIA SECURE?

Private Internet Access is pretty impenetrable when it comes to privacy. However, what about safety? PIA helps to keep you safe and anonymous online by encrypting your internet traffic and hiding your IP address. When you connect to the internet through PIA's VPN service, your internet traffic is routed through one of PIA's servers, where it is encrypted and securely transmitted to your destination. This makes it difficult for others to monitor or access your internet activity, as your internet traffic appears to be coming from PIA's server rather than from your device.

PIA also supports multiple VPN protocols, allowing you to choose the best protocol for your needs and requirements. The available protocols may vary depending on the device and platform you are using, so be sure to check the supported protocols for your device before selecting a VPN protocol.

The protocols used by PIA include:

  • PPTP (Point-to-Point Tunneling Protocol): PPTP is an older VPN protocol that is still widely used and provides fast speeds. However, it is not as secure as some other VPN protocols.

  • L2TP/IPSec (Layer 2 Tunneling Protocol with Internet Protocol Security): L2TP/IPSec is a newer and more secure VPN protocol that provides a high level of encryption for your internet connection.

  • OpenVPN: OpenVPN is an open-source VPN protocol that provides a high level of encryption and security for your internet connection. It is the recommended VPN protocol for most users as it provides fast speeds and strong security.

  • WireGuard: WireGuard is a new and modern VPN protocol that provides a fast and secure connection. It is designed to be lightweight, simple, and efficient, making it an ideal VPN protocol for users who need a fast and reliable VPN connection.

    Private Internet Access also uses RAM-only servers for its network, which is great for privacy. Instead of using hard drives, which store data and need to be wiped manually, RAM servers are designed to clear all data when they are restarted automatically. This means that with the flick of a switch, all stored data is removed, providing a much higher level of privacy and security.

    Overall, PIA's dedication to transparency and user privacy is one of the reasons it is regarded as one of the most reliable VPN providers on the market.
