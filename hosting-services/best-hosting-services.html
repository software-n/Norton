Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


What is a hosting service?

A hosting service offers a web-based interface that allows authorized users to manage servers, hosted services, websites, databases, files, and email accounts.

Web hosting providers help maintain their clients’ websites and databases and give them access to manage their sites, including backups, security, add-on installations, file uploads, and bandwidth monitoring.



Why would I need a hosting service?

No one can see or visit your website if you don’t host it on a server. Web hosting companies provide the necessary space and infrastructure for your website to go live and attract visitors. They also have an IT team on board to handle the technical side of website maintenance and ensure everything runs smoothly.

Your provider’s hosting software enables you to optimize your website’s performance and enhance security. You can install and manage security plugins, firewalls, spam prevention tools, data encryption, and SSL certificates.

You can gain insights into your website traffic (ideal for scaling) and save time, money, and resources configuring and running your site. You don’t have to worry about losing data or specific website content after updates, as you can back up your site and data effortlessly.

Your hosting provider will ensure your site is stable and provide technical support to fix issues promptly. You can access that support on the control panel.



What can I do with a hosting service?

A good hosting service should be able to give you control over your website management, enabling you to:

  • Connect multiple domains to the server;
  • Configure and manage branded email accounts;
  • Install a CMS (Content Management System) on your hosting plan;
  • Upload files to the server;
  • Install add-ons to your site;
  • Create and manage databases;
  • Back up your website;
  • Monitor and adjust the bandwidth;
  • Modify security settings.

    • You can perform these and other tasks by logging directly into the server, but you must have expert knowledge and experience. A hosting service doesn’t require that expertise; anyone can understand how to use it in no time.

    What features to look for in a hosting provider?

    A reliable hosting service is essential to managing your website and its hosting environment. Here are the top features to look for when choosing your hosting provider:

  • Domain and subdomain management - Find a hosting company that lets you create and manage these elements to separate and organize your content.

  • Suitable server space - Determine the storage amount you’ll need for images, audio and video files, textual content, and other website elements.

  • Bandwidth - This feature is the amount of traffic and resources your website has during a specific period. Choose your hosting software provider according to your website’s size and expected visitors to ensure optimal performance.

  • DNS management - Look for this feature if you want to store multiple domains on a single server. It will enable you to customize each.

  • Add-ons - Most hosting providers offer various add-ons to help clients optimize or safeguard their websites. They include unlimited storage, unlimited bandwidth, drag-and-drop website builders, free domains (typically for one year), DDoS attack prevention, ecommerce merchant accounts, PCI-compliant hosting, firewalls, VPNs, load balancers, etc.

  • File management - The service should allow you to upload, download, and delete files from your server.

  • Mailbox management - This feature isn’t a must-have; look for it if you need a business email hosting service.

  • Database management - This feature lets you create new and manage existing databases from a centralized location.

  • Top-notch security - Website security is crucial, so check your shortlisted providers’ settings and functionalities for preventing cyberattacks and safeguarding site content and data.

  • Uptime guarantee - Find a provider guaranteeing nearly 100% uptime to ensure optimal performance.

  • User-friendly interface - Your hosting service should save you time and help you manage your website seamlessly.

  • Server compatibility - Ensure your web server is compatible with your operating system.

  • Excellent customer support - Professionals should be available 24/7 to resolve any technical issues and keep your website up and running.
