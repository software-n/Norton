Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


What are website builders?

Website builders are special tools that allow users to create websites without coding knowledge or experience, and they can be broken down into two main categories – online and offline.

Online website builders are web-based, allowing you to create a new website using the company’s server. You just need a decent WiFi connection, and you can immediately start creating your website.

Offline website builders are software programs that are installed on a personal computer. Once they’re downloaded and installed, you don’t have to be connected to the internet to work on your website. Instead, all features are available offline.

Both online and offline website builders have their pros and cons, so it all comes down to personal preference, which one you should pick.

Thanks to their ease of use, website builders are perfect for freelancers and small business owners with a limited budget who can’t afford to launch a full-blown professional website but still need something to improve their online presence.



What can website builders do?

When it comes to website builders, only the sky’s the limit. Website builders can do many different things, but their primary purpose is to help you enhance your online presence. Since building a website is recommended for companies from all industries, website builders can do anything from creating a business site or web shop to getting a basic type of blog running.

    • Why would I need website builders?

    If you want to launch a website but don’t have any coding experience or resources to outsource this task to a professional, using a website builder is the next best thing.

    Website builders can be free or paid. However, even when they come with a fee, they are a much cheaper version compared to hiring a professional, so you can put together an aesthetically pleasing and highly functional site without breaking the bank.

    Website builders come with a large selection of pre-made templates, thus allowing you to create a polished and functional website even if you don’t have any experience in coding. Furthermore, the themes are usually customizable, so you can easily create a unique website that perfectly fits your brand.



    What features do quality website builders have?

    Most website builders have the following features you can use:

  • Drag-and-drop interface: With this convenient feature, you can easily drag and drop website components you’d like to incorporate into your website design. Whether you want to insert a piece of text, photo, video, or document, having all crucial components in one place is simple with the drag-and-drop interface.

  • SEO analytics: SEO is the latest trend that must be a part of your website. SEO deals with increasing internet traffic, so having access to such data can be highly beneficial to building a stronger online presence.

  • Mobile compatibility: Having a website that only looks good on a large computer monitor is pretty useless today. Most people use mobile devices, and mobile compatibility is the key to allowing all your visitors to browse your website seamlessly.

  • Business email: Most website builders will give you the possibility of creating a business email. Personal and business emails aren’t the same because business emails use your domain name as the extension.
