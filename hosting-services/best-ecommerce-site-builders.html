Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


What are eCommerce website builders?

eCommerce website builders are tools and platforms that enable you to create your own online store site from scratch. User-friendly and intuitive, they don’t require any programming knowledge or coding skills – you can use them to their fullest effect even if you’re a complete beginner.

Most commonly, they have drag-and-drop functionality that enables you to customize virtually every aspect of your site without much fuss. You can seamlessly create your preferred layout, make use of the hundreds of freely available themes, and embed any feature you’d like into your eCommerce website.

Of course, they come with built-in eCommerce capabilities that enable you to take online orders and process payments with ease.

As a general rule of thumb, web-based eCommerce website builders are more beginner-friendly and, therefore, easier to master. All you need is an internet connection, and you can sign in on your preferred platform and start building your online store.

Offline eCommerce website builders require more technical expertise. You’d download the software to your computer and start building your site directly through the program – you don’t need an internet connection, and you can launch your site whenever you’re ready.



What do eCommerce website builders do?

eCommerce website builders enable you to establish a strong online presence that attracts customers. These tools allow you to transform your existing site into an eCommerce store or even build an entire online store from scratch.

With an eCommerce site builder, you can create a comprehensive site containing everything you and your online customers need – detailed product pages, a seamless shopping experience, smooth online transactions, and more.

A reliable builder will allow you to optimize your site for search engines, improve your page loading speed, attract higher website traffic, and generate more leads. After all, if you want to outperform the competitors, you need an attractive, functional eCommerce website that draws the interest of your audiences. Unless you’re a web developer, you can only achieve this with an eCommerce website builder.



What are the benefits of using eCommerce website builders?

Using eCommerce website builders offers an array of benefits, being an especially attractive solution for small and medium-sized business owners across industries and niches. Whether you’re a retailer, blogger, artist, hand-made accessory creator, or anything in between, you can benefit from using these builders.

With them, you can create a full-blown online store in a matter of hours, even if you have no prior experience doing anything similar. Their simple drag-and-drop functionality makes them easy to master, so you can seamlessly include any feature you deem necessary. And if the builder is lacking a feature, you can always get a few plugins to enhance the capabilities of your site.

What’s more, you can save thousands of dollars in the process. eCommerce website builders often have an affordable price that won’t harm your budget, and there are many free solutions you can take advantage of, as well.



What features should I look for in eCommerce website builders?

When choosing your eCommerce website builder, you’ll need to pay special attention to the following features:

  • Drag-and-drop capabilities;
  • Mobile-optimization options;
  • SEO analytics;
  • Traffic analytics;
  • Payment processing capabilities;
  • Inventory tracking;
  • CRM integration;
  • SSL security;
  • Coupon creation tools;
  • Social media integration;
  • Live chat functionality.

    • These features will help you simplify processes, improve customer satisfaction, and grow your online store.
