WHAT DO THEY OFFER?

After launching in 1996, Bluehost has expanded to become a global powerhouse in the web hosting industry. More than 2 million websites in the globe are presently running on it. Bluehost has had a long relationship with the WordPress community. In fact, they have been an official WordPress.org recommended web host for over a decade. Today, Bluehost offers a wide range of hosting-related products, from domain names to dedicated servers. Since you’re probably here mainly to compare hosting services, let's quickly look at what Bluehost has to offer:

  • Shared hosting - A form of web hosting service in which numerous websites share a physical Web server and its resources. It's great for a new website that doesn't have many visitors yet.

  • VPS hosting - VPS, or virtual private server hosting, is ideal for growing business that need more hosting capabilities than basic shared hosting but are not yet ready for dedicated hosting on a private server. VPS hosting provides the customization, security, and control of a dedicated server at the cost of a shared server.

  • Dedicated hosting - Dedicated hosting allows you to have an entire server for your website, applications, and business – all to yourself. This is the fastest and most reliable hosting model offered by Bluehost. It delivers tremendous power and adaptability, but it comes at a price. This platform is great for medium- to large-sized firms, developers, IT executives, system administrators, and information technology firms.

  • Cloud hosting - With cloud hosting, your website is kept on many servers, allowing you to access resources from various locations. This makes cloud hosting a highly scalable, stable, and adaptable form of hosting, ideal for sites that undergo traffic spikes and dips.

  • WooCommerce hosting - WooCommerce makes it simple to turn a WordPress site into an online store. This free and open-source plugin offers eCommerce functionalities to any self-hosted WordPress site, whether you're a one-person shop selling a few items or a retail business with big ambitions. A shared hosting package will get you up and running with your WooCommerce store. Still, if you want to grow your business and boost your brand, a dedicated WooCommerce hosting will give you the security, speed, and flexibility you need.

  • WordPress hosting - Their WordPress hosting plans are created exclusively for websites powered by WordPress. It is optimized for WordPress and can safeguard your WordPress website from typical attacks.


    • HOW SECURE IS IT?

    Bluehost shines in terms of security. Spam Hammer, Spam Experts, and Apache Spam Assassin provide three tiers of anti-spam security. You also receive inbox protection, all of your directories are password-protected, and it's simple to set up filters. Do you need to restrict access to your website? You can go ahead and blacklist IP addresses. Secure shell (SSH) access is another outstanding feature. Thanks to this, you can now safely access configuration files separately. In addition, Bluehost now includes one-click integration with CloudFlare, which is especially effective at preventing DDOS assaults. This helps secure and speed up your site even more!


    IS IT FAST AND RELIABLE?

    Bluehost does a good job in terms of performance. It might not be the fastest web host on the market, but you can count on it to be very reliable. In our tests, Bluehost's servers consistently delivered results in the 376ms range, demonstrating their blazing fast speed. For shared hosting, a response time of up to 400 milliseconds is considered excellent. Bluehost doesn't oversell its shared hosting servers. Some low-quality hosting companies do this to save money, but Bluehost doesn't.

    Faster speeds are possible thanks to the inclusion of CloudFlair services in various Bluehost hosting packages, in addition to the already excellent servers. Over the past 30 days, Bluehost's servers boasted an amazing average uptime of 99.764%. Average uptime is greater than 99.96%. Simply put, if you host your site with Bluehost, you can expect it to be up and operating almost all of the time.


    EASE OF USE

    Establishing and maintaining your website is simple with Bluehost. The improved cPanel interface is extremely simple to use—all you have to do is click on the icons to navigate. With your websites and email addresses in one place, you can manage everything. The control panel's design can also be altered to suit your requirements. This makes it easy to access your preferred functionalities.


    PRICING

    Bluehost provides a variety of straightforward shared hosting solutions. Right away, you'll note that they don't provide a month-to-month subscription. Bluehost's web hosting packages come in one-year and three-year durations, depending on your commitment level. The cost can be as low as $2.95 for a Basic plan or as high as $13.95 for a Pro plan.Take note that these prices are for a shared hosting plan, Bluehost has a different pricing scheme for dedicated hosting, VPS, and website plans.


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    We all require assistance from time to time, so it's critical that you select a web hosting provider that provides assistance and support when you require it. The good thing is that Bluehost offers a variety of support options. To begin, it offers an extensive knowledge base that comprises a large library of articles, step-by-step instructions, and video lessons. This is ideal for novices and people who want to do their own research. If you want to talk to a person, though, you can do that too. Bluehost offers phone and live chat support that is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week. This is very helpful when you need technical support right away, and know that the help you need is just a click away.


    VERDICT

    Bluehost has shown in this review that it is all about the user's experience. The hosting packages offered by Bluehost are also reasonably priced, all-inclusive, and offer incredibly easy site maintenance and quick, dependable performance. All of these things make the service good for everyone, whether you're a beginner, an expert, or a busy professional. We recommend Bluehost for local small businesses, entrepreneurs, bloggers, e-commerce sites, and anyone looking for a dependable web host.
