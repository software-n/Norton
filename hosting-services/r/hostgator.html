WHAT DO THEY OFFER?

Since its humble beginnings in 2002, HostGator has established itself as a global leader in the web hosting industry. It provides a number of hosting services such as:

  • Shared hosting - Their shared hosting packages are perfect for low to medium traffic websites that don't need dedicated resources. This service provides space for multiple tenants (websites) on a single server, analogous to roommates in a household. Probably the one you ought to purchase, at least initially.

  • VPS hosting - If you need more power than what shared hosting can provide, consider upgrading to a virtual private server (VPS). As with shared hosting, a VPS hosting server will house multiple websites, but significantly fewer than those hosted on a shared hosting server. You will pay more for a VPS server, but you will also receive more server power.

  • Dedicated hosting - Dedicated hosting refers to web hosting plans that offer a dedicated server with dedicated resources to a single client. This type of hosting is ideal for midsize to large organizations with 500 or more employees that manage vast quantities of data. A dedicated server is required if your business has databases, a large inventory/supply chain infrastructure, and hundreds of ecommerce transactions per hour.

  • Cloud hosting - With cloud hosting, your website's required resources are distributed across multiple servers. The main benefit? Your site is not constrained by physical server limitations, so you can scale resources in real time.

  • WordPress Hosting - You can install the WordPress content management system (CMS) on any of HostGator's servers. This hosting package provides twice the speed of the competition, advanced security, free migrations, and free SSL certificates.

  • Website Builder - You can also create professional-looking websites with HostGator. Their website builder is equipped with a flexible drag-and-drop page editor and more than 200 pre-designed layouts. It even has eCommerce capabilities! If you want to launch your site even more quickly, you can utilize their AI-enabled website builder.

  • Reseller Hosting - A reseller account allows you to launch your own web hosting company, complete with your own domain name, customer service department, and billing system. Behind the scenes, HostGator acts as your supplier, but to your clients, you are the hosting provider, not HostGator.


    • HOW SECURE IS IT?

    HostGator provides a free SSL certificate that protects your website domains and encrypts communications between the server and website visitors. They also provide additional security features such as data backup, fraud prevention, and the ability to add SiteLock monitoring to your site. This service will run daily, scanning for potential site breaches or hacks and notifying you immediately if they find one. Overall, HostGator provides a number of powerful security features that aid in the protection of your data. While no guarantees can be made that your data will never be compromised, HostGator's security measures are extremely effective and should be used by all customers.


    IS IT FAST AND RELIABLE?

    HostGator guarantees a 99.9% uptime on their servers. Shared and reseller hosting plans are covered by this guarantee, and if they don't deliver, you may be eligible for a one month credit. That demonstrates their level of faith in their capacity to keep your websites operational 99.9% of the time. In addition, while we aren't crazy about how the majority of HostGator's data centers are located in the United States, we have zero issues with the speed at which their servers respond.


    EASE OF USE

HostGator's user-friendly interface and speedy provisioning system make getting started a breeze.
