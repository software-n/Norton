WHAT DO THEY OFFER?

DreamHost is one of the oldest web hosting services, having been around since 1996. They currently host over 1.5 million websites, 750,000 WordPress installations, and 400,000 customers from more than 100 countries. Although it may not enjoy the same level of public awareness as some of its rivals, the brand is remarkably well-known among programmers, designers, and web marketers. DreamHost has many features, including one-click site migration, automated backups, an improved control panel, business solutions, and more. What about web hosting? Here are your options:

  • Managed WordPress Hosting - With managed WordPress hosting, the technical parts of your site are taken care of for you. This includes security, speed, WordPress updates, daily backups, uptime, and more. A managed hosting provider could help your business if you have a big website that gets a lot of traffic.DreamHost's Managed WordPress Hosting can get you up and running quickly, as it is optimized for performance and preconfigured for easy maintenance.

  • Shared Website Hosting - With this entry-level web hosting, you can get your website up and running in no time. In addition to the free domain name and fast SSD storage, email hosting, website builder, and Free SSL Certificate, you also get a bunch of other perks. Keep in mind that if your website gets more than 50,000 visitors a month, it might be time to switch to a dedicated server.

  • VPS Hosting - Virtual Private Server (VPS) plans are like a shared hosting server that has been partitioned into multiple smaller servers. You can adjust the settings to suit your preferences. Your website will be hosted on a server that is completely separate from those of any other clients, with its own dedicated set of resources. DreamHost's VPS hosting plans feature lightning-fast SSDs and a wide range of software and protocol support, including HTTP/2, Ubuntu, IPv6, Nginx, Node.js, and more.

  • WP Website Builder - Creating your own website has never been easier thanks to DreamHost's website builder. You'll have access to thousands of premium templates and powerful plugins, and the drag-and-drop tools eliminate the need for coding knowledge. This plan is ideal for bloggers, business owners, and e-commerce novices who want to get their content and business online as soon as possible.

  • Dedicated Hosting - This type of hosting is appropriate if you have a gaming, streaming, or remote working platform, a high-traffic website, or a company that manages a large amount of data. DreamHosts currently offers two dedicated hosting plans, both of which include Root Access, a local MySQL database server, DDoS protection, 24x7 tech support, and server monitoring, among other things.

  • Cloud Hosting - For those who demand flexibility and transparency from their host, cloud hosting provides you with a scalable, secure, and quick hosting option. This solution is perfect if you anticipate spikes in traffic because you can add more resources (like RAM and CPU) as you go. DreamHost offers two cloud-based services: DreamObjects and DreamCompute.

    • DreamHost does a lot more than just host websites. They also offer web development, social media marketing, SEO marketing, technical website management, and design services, making it a one-stop shop for businesses.


    HOW SECURE IS IT?

    Each plan comes with a lot of free security tools that keep your site safe from unencrypted connections and malware. This includes things like domain privacy, SSL certificates, daily backups, and multi-factor authentication. DreamHost also has security tools built in, like mod security, which is a Web Application Firewall (WAF) that filters and blocks HTTP requests that are known to be harmful. They also have a service called DreamShield that checks for malware. This feature costs extra, but it's worth it because it lets you look for malware on your website. DreamHost also lets you block spiders from parts of your website that you don't want search engines to index or don't need them to. There's more if that wasn't enough. DreamHost gives you access to SSH and SFTP, and it also works with HTTP/2 and PHP 7.


    IS IT FAST AND RELIABLE?

    DreamHost has a stellar reputation for providing lightning-quick service and superb uptime. Both experts and consumers alike consistently give them the highest possible ratings and praise. DreamHost is also quite possibly the only low-cost host that is bold enough to guarantee 100% uptime. In fact, it is expressly stated in their General Terms of Service that "A failure to provide 100% uptime will result in customer compensation pursuant to guidelines established herein" Our tests also demonstrated that this isn't merely a pretense. We haven't experienced a single service interruption in the few months we've been using this provider. Response and page load times were also satisfactory. We recommend DreamHost to anyone looking for a reliable and affordable web host.


    EASE OF USE

    DreamHost, unlike many other web hosts, does not use the cPanel software. Instead, you will be given a personalized control panel. If you're concerned that you won't be able to navigate it, don't worry! DreamHost turned out to be one of the easier web hosts to use, and part of the reason is that it doesn't have too many features, so it's easy to find your way around.


    PRICING

    DreamHost provides a variety of pricing plans that correspond to various types of web hosting. There are only two options for shared hosting, however, which simplifies your purchasing decision.

    Shared Starter

    • 1 Website
    • Free Domain included
    • Unlimited Traffic
    • WordPress Pre-Installed
    • Free Automated WordPress Migrations
    • WP Website Builder
    • Fast SSD Storage
    • Free SSL Certificate
    • Add Email as low as $1.67/mo
    • 3-Year, 1 Year & Monthly Plans

    Shared Unlimited

    • Same inclusion with the Shared Starter with the following differences:
    • Unlimited Websites
    • Unlimited Email @ Your Domain


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    DreamHost's website features a forum where you can post your questions and a Knowledge base where you can find answers. Most forum questions are answered by the support staff within 24 hours. They also provide ticket-based email support anytime, day or night. In the Help section of your DreamHost control panel, you can submit a ticket asking for assistance. Additionally, the panel provides access to live Chat support from 5:30 AM to 9:30 PM PST. For an additional fee, you can also request a callback. As evidenced by DreamHost's near-perfect TrustPilot rating (4.7/5) and mountain of raving reviews of their customer service, you can rest easy knowing you're in good hands.


    VERDICT

    Overall, DreamHost is an excellent choice for anyone looking to host a website, whether you are a small or large business. There are numerous plans to choose from, each with a plethora of features at an affordable price. Furthermore, their default plan limits are quite high, with a slew of "unlimited" options for disk space and bandwidth, free domain, and other features. Additionally, site migration is now free for WordPress users.
