COMPANY BACKGROUND

Wix.com is a cloud-based website builder founded in 2006 by Avishai Abrahami, Nadav Abrahami, and Giora Kaplan. The company is headquartered in Tel Aviv, Israel, and has offices in several other countries around the world, including the United States and Germany.

Wix.com offers a range of customizable website templates and an intuitive drag-and-drop editor, making it easy for users with no technical knowledge to create professional-looking websites. The platform also provides advanced features such as e-commerce tools, marketing and SEO tools, and third-party app integrations.

Over the years, Wix.com has grown to become one of the most popular website builders in the world, with over 200 million users in 190 countries. The company has also expanded its offerings to include additional products and services, such as a logo maker, business management tools, and website design services.

Wix.com has received numerous awards and recognitions over the years, including being named a "Leader" in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Website Builders and being named "Best Overall Website Builder" by TechRadar in 2020.

    HOW SECURE IS IT?

    Wix.com takes security very seriously and employs a number of measures to keep user data and websites secure. Here are some of the security features and practices offered by Wix.com:

  • SSL encryption - Wix.com provides SSL encryption for all websites hosted on the platform, which helps to protect sensitive data such as login credentials and payment information.

  • Data protection - Wix.com complies with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and other data protection laws, ensuring that user data is protected and handled responsibly.

  • Server security - Wix.com uses industry-standard security protocols and firewalls to protect its servers and prevent unauthorized access.

  • Automatic backups - Wix.com automatically backs up all websites hosted on the platform on a regular basis, which helps to ensure that user data is not lost in the event of a security breach or other issue.

  • Password protection - Wix.com requires users to create strong passwords and offers tools to help users manage and reset their passwords.

  • Two-factor authentication - Wix.com offers two-factor authentication, which adds an extra layer of security by requiring users to provide a second form of authentication, such as a code sent to their mobile device.


    • PRICING

    Wix.com offers a range of pricing plans to fit different budgets and website needs. Here is an overview of Wix.com's pricing and plans:

  • Free Plan - Wix.com offers a free plan with limited features, including 500MB storage and bandwidth, and Wix ads on your site.

  • Connect Domain - This plan starts at $4.50 per month and includes 1GB bandwidth, 500MB storage, and the ability to connect a custom domain name to your site.

  • Combo Plan - This plan starts at $16 per month and includes 2GB bandwidth, 3GB storage, 30 minutes of video, a free domain for one year, and the ability to remove Wix ads from your site. This is best for personal use only.

  • Unlimited Plan - This plan starts at $22 per month and includes unlimited bandwidth, 5GB storage, 1 video hour, and the ability to add a form builder app or site booster app. This plan is ideal for entrepreneurs and freelancers.

  • Pro Plan - Considered as the best-value plan for complete online branding, this plan starts at $27 per month and includes priority support, unlimited bandwidth, 50GB storage, 2 video hours, as well as the ability to add a professional logo to your site.

  • VIP Plan - This plan starts at $45 per month and includes priority support, unlimited bandwidth, 100GB storage, 5 video hours, and the ability to add a professional logo and social media logo files.

  • Business Basic Plan - This plan starts at $27 per month and includes e-commerce features, such as the ability to accept payments, manage products, and track orders.

  • Business Unlimited Plan - This plan starts at $32 per month and includes all the features of the Business Basic plan, plus additional storage, a professional logo, and the ability to send automated abandoned cart recovery emails.

  • Business VIP Plan - This plan starts at $59 per month and includes all the features of the Business Unlimited plan, plus priority support and the ability to create and send professional invoices.

    • It's important to note that the prices listed above are for annual subscriptions, and the monthly price may be higher for month-to-month subscriptions. Additionally, some advanced features and add-ons may come with additional fees. Overall, Wix.com's pricing and plans offer a range of options for users with different budgets and website needs.

Wix.com also offers a dedicated support team for e-commerce users, providing additional assistance with setting up and managing online stores.
