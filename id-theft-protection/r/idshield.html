FEATURES

IDShield has a reputation for offering comprehensive credit and identity monitoring services for families and individuals. When using this service, it’s easy to spot early signs of identity theft and take measures to prevent it from ever happening. IDShield will monitor all the major credit bureaus and alert you if any changes or new activities appear on your report.

This service has various features that set it apart from other companies. Here is an overview of some of the features of IDShield Identity Theft Protection.

  • Identity Monitoring: IDShield will monitor your Social Security number, tax ID, email addresses, and other personal information on social media platforms and the dark web. You'll get alerts in case of malicious activities. The best thing is that IDShield will help you monitor your children's identities to ensure fraudsters are not targeting them.

  • Credit Monitoring: The services will help you stay on top of your credit health by monitoring all three credit bureaus. You can view your credit score and report with IDShield.

  • Identity Restoration: If you become a victim of identity theft, a licensed private investigator will work with you to restore your identity. This includes helping you cancel accounts, file police reports, and more.

  • Full Support: You're entitled to full support from a team of licensed private investigators if your identity is under threat. They'll commit to your case until you get a solution.

  • Insurance Coverage: IDShield offers up to $1 million in insurance coverage for any expenses related to identity theft. The costs include legal fees, lost wages, and more.

  • Device Protection: IDShield can add an extra layer of protection for your devices through a VPN, parental controls, malware protection, and password protection. With a VPN, your data is encrypted so no one can hack into your device or see what you're doing online. Parental controls will help you monitor and manage your children's online activity.

  • Lost Wallet Support: If you lose your wallet, IDShield will help you assess whether someone has used your information to commit fraud. Experts will also help you cancel and replace all your credit cards.


    • PRICING AND PLANS

    IDShield offers two pricing plans: The individual plan and the family plan.

    The individual plan protects only one individual, and pricing for the plan starts at $14.95 per month. With this plan, you will get credit monitoring for only one credit bureau. However, you'll have access to all the other features, including identity monitoring, identity restoration, lost wallet support, and more.

    The higher individual pricing plan goes for $19.95 per month. This plan will get you credit monitoring for all three credit bureaus and all other IDShield Identity Theft Protection features. On the other hand, the family plan protects you, your domestic partner or spouse, and any of your dependents under 18 years of age. The price of the family plan starts at $29.95/month, with only one credit bureau monitoring. The other family plan will cost you $34.95 per month, and you will have three credit bureaus monitored.

    You and your household will get more comprehensive monitoring of your credit files and reports. IDShield will help you track your new credit applications, outstanding debt, and payment history. You will know when someone tries to use your personal information to open a new account or make a major purchase.


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    IDShield has a supportive customer service team that's always ready to help you. The team is available 24/7, so you can always get help when you need it. You can reach them via phone or email, and they'll respond to your inquiry as soon as possible. If social anxiety always gets in your way, you can connect with a customer support representative through the company's live chat feature. You can also find a lot of helpful information on the IDShield website. The website has an extensive FAQ section covering all the service's features. There's also a blog with informative articles on identity theft, credit monitoring, and more.
