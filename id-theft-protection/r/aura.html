FEATURES

Aura is a full-service software when it comes to digital and financial security. Users get necessary security features like VPN, antivirus, and password protection. Aura’s strength, though, lies in its identity theft, credit fraud, and financial protection features that allow you to control your personal information and identity.

  • Online and Device Security - Aura offers the expected foundational security features to keep your identity, information, and devices protected. This includes antivirus software that automatically detects and isolates malware threats, as well as spyware, ransomware, and adware.

  • VPN Protection - The Aura Online Security features include a built-in VPN with military-grade encryption. This feature is especially geared towards protecting your online activity and personal information when using public WiFi.

  • Password Manager - With increasingly higher standards for password security, Aura’s Password Manager makes the process of generating secure passwords and managing them automatic. That means you don’t have to worry about creating stronger passwords (it does it for you) or remembering them. When the Password Manager detects a potential breach, it can automatically update your password for the site to keep your account and personal information secure.

  • Safe Browsing - When using the Safe Browsing feature, Aura can detect suspicious scam or phishing sites and protect you from entering them.

  • Identity Theft Protection - Prevent identity theft before it happens with this feature that monitors your social security number and financial accounts for any signs of fraud or a security breach. You’ll receive alerts if any potential issues arise.

  • Spam and Robocall Reduction - This feature helps users identify data brokers that have your personal information. You can put in an opt-out request through Aura and it will request that your data be removed so that it won’t be sold to advertisers and telemarketers in the future.

  • Credit Protection - Aura allows you to access, protect, and somewhat control your Experian credit file. Users have the ability to instantly place a lock on their credit file that prevents any inquiries into your Experian credit. Additionally, you can view your Experian credit score every month. You’ll also get alerts for any nex credit inquiries as they happen to make you aware of potential fraud.

  • Financial Fraud Protection - Aura allows you to connect your bank accounts so that it can detect and alert you of suspicious charges to your account. You can set spending alerts and receive notifications when transactions go above them.


    • PRICING AND PLANS

    Aura plans are based on the number of users who want to make use of the software. You have the option to pay monthly or annually for a discount. The three plans currently available are:

    • Individual - One member subscription. $12/mo billed annually, $15/mo billed monthly.
    • Couple - Two member subscription. $22/mo billed annually, $29/mo billed monthly.
    • Family - Five member subscription. $37/mo billed annually, $50/mo billed monthly.

    Every plan offers a 14-day free trial. If you purchase an annual plan, you have the option to ask for your money back within 60 days of your initial purchase date if you end up not being satisfied with the software.

    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    Aura’s customer support is one of the selling points of the software, with 24/7 access to live chat and live phone customer support services. Its White Glove fraud resolution team, which helps you navigate recovering your identity in the case of fraud, goes above and beyond the usual call of duty for security softwares. They also have people who submit requests on your behalf to remove your information from calling and spam lists.
