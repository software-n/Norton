FEATURES

Panda Dome Passwords may not be packed with features like Nordpass, and RoboForm, but it's an excellent choice for those who want a safe and fuss-free way to keep track of their passwords. Here's what makes this password manager simple yet effective:

  • Password Generator - Forget using birthdates, anniversaries, pet names, or any other personal information when creating your password. After all, passwords are your first line of defense against hackers and any other intruders. Use Panda Dome's password generator to generate strong and unique passwords that you don't have to remember. These passwords range from 4 to 32 characters and combine upper and lower case letters, numbers, and special characters.

  • Password Database - Panda Dome's password manager offers unlimited password storage on multiple devices.

  • Identities - The password database also lets you store credit card details and login identities or personal information like your name, address, and phone number. This way, when you have to check out while shopping online, you won't have to whip out your credit card or manually type anything ever again. This feature also allows you to create multiple identities, which is great if you use separate work and personal details.

  • Password Capture - With too many password-protected websites and applications, complex password requirements, and forced password reset, it's hard to stay on top of it all. Seriously, how many times did you have to reset passwords this year? Fortunately, you won't have to memorize or write your passwords on sticky notes anymore. When you visit a site, Panda Dome offers to capture your credentials, so it can log you in automatically when you visit the same domain next time.


    • ARE THERE ANY EXTRAS?

    To further protect your login credentials, Panda's password manager offers the following features:

  • Security Report - Combines what other password managers call "password strength checker" or "breach monitor." This feature lets you check if any of your passwords are weak, duplicates, or has been leaked or compromised.

  • Secure Me - Misplacing your smartphone, or worse, having it stolen, is not only nerve-wracking. It is also downright dangerous. What if your data falls into the wrong hands? Luckily, there's "Secure Me" — a feature that can remotely log you out from all websites, delete all tabs, and clear cookies and browsing histories, all with a single click. You can also disconnect suspicious devices that you don't even recognize. It's not just for misplaced and stolen devices, either. If you've ever left your desktop computer running while you step out of the office for a quick coffee run, it is comforting to know that you can sign out remotely while you order that soy latte.


    • IS IT SAFE AND RELIABLE?

    Security is probably the biggest selling point of this password manager. After all, it comes from the same cybersecurity company that brought us Panda Security — a leading antivirus software known for its machine learning and big data technology. For starters, Panda Dome protects its password storage with high-level encryption. It also supports several authentication apps, including Google Authenticator, Duo Mobile, Authy, and SoundLogin. In addition, you can enable fingerprint authentication or set up a 4-digit PIN code. Lastly, this password manager operates on a strict zero-knowledge policy so that no one, not even Panda Security, can access any of your stored data.


    COMPATIBLE PLATFORMS AND EASE OF USE

    One thing you'll notice is that the desktop web browser extension's interface looks almost identical to their mobile apps. The icons and features are displayed in the same place to prevent user confusion. It's good news for those who hate learning to navigate yet another app on their phone. Overall, we find Panda's user interface clean, simple, and easy to navigate. Panda Dome Passwords is available as Chrome, Edge, and Firefox browser extensions and has mobile apps for OS and Android devices.


    PRICING AND PLANS

    You can try Panda Dome Passwords free for a month, but only if you get it bundled with Panda Dome Premium — a full-service antivirus suite that also includes a VPN. You can also purchase it as a standalone app for as low as $18.39 for 12 months ($1.53/month). As a subscription goes, you can save more if you sign up for 2 or 3 years. A 30-day money-back guarantee backs every purchase of Panda Dome Passwords, whether bundled or standalone.


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    If you need help with anything, you can visit Panda Security's support page, but frankly, it isn't very helpful. We found just four password-related articles, and these are not technical. The forum is also not active, with some threads dating back years, so you'll probably have more luck sending a message to their Twitter account as the admin updates a few times each day. Don't have Twitter? No problem. You can send them a ticket, and a customer service agent will reach out to you via email. If you prefer a phone call, you can indicate that in your message and leave your phone number in the "detailed description" field.
