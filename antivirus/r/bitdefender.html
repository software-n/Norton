WHO IS BEHIND BITDEFENDER?

Bitdefender is a Romanian cybersecurity and anti-virus software company, founded in 2001 by Florin Talpes. The company was initially focused on developing anti-virus software for personal computers. Over time, it expanded its product line to include internet security, endpoint security, and other cybersecurity products and services.

In 2017, Bitdefender was acquired by a private equity firm, Francisco Partners, and is now a privately held company. The company has offices in several countries, including Romania, the United States, Germany, and the United Kingdom.

The internet security software provides additional protection for internet-connected devices, including firewalls, spam filters, and parental controls. WHAT ARE THE FEATURES?

Bitdefender utilizes a combination of behavioral and heuristic detection technologies to provide complete and real-time protection against all types of threats: viruses, worms, Trojan, adware, spyware, zero-day exploits, rootkits, and ransomware. From botnet-related URLs to brute force attacks, it also protects at a network level to stop the intrusion before it happens.

Bitdefender offers a wide range of features depending on the product or service. These features can be divided into three categories: Protection, Privacy, and Performance. Let’s go through all the features under the Protection category first:


Protection:

  • Safe Files- This is your first and best defense against ransomware attacks. Ransomware is a type of malware that can encrypt your file and hold it "hostage" until you pay for decryption. Safe Files prevents this situation by alerting you and blocking unauthorized access and modifications of your files, apps, and folders, including your photos, videos, documents, and more.

  • Network Threat Prevention- This new cyber threat intelligence technology can analyze and find suspicious network-level activities, as well as block sophisticated exploits, URLs related to malware and botnets, and brute force attacks.

  • Advanced Threat Defense- Bitdefender closely monitors running apps using a method known as behavioral detection. It immediately takes action to stop infections the moment it notices anything suspicious.

  • Multi-Layer Ransomware Protection- When it comes to protecting your data from ransomware, Bitdefender's multi-layered approach includes a data-protection layer that keeps your files safe no matter the type of attack. This includes your photos, videos, personal and work documents, music, and other data files. By integrating with Advanced Threat Defense, it helps prevent ransomware from encrypting your most crucial data.

  • Vulnerability Assessment- This feature identifies and alerts you to vulnerabilities in your system that could be exploited by hackers, and helps you to fix them.

  • Firewall- The firewall lets you keep an eye on the connections your apps make and set different rules for each one. You can choose, for example, what kind of network (home or public) they can connect to, what kind of protocol they can use, and whether they can make inbound or outbound connections (or both). And if that wasn't enough, you can also set up your own IPs and Ports. Last, you can turn on or off protection against port scans and stealth mode.

  • Web Attack Prevention- This ensures that you never visit a malicious website. Before clicking on a link, you should determine whether your search results are secure. Additionally, Bitdefender helps block known infected links.

  • Anti-Phishing- There are more and more online scams, but Bitdefender's advanced anti-phishing protection keeps you safe. This feature looks for websites that pretend to be safe in order to steal financial information like passwords or credit card numbers. If it finds one, it blocks it.

  • Anti-Fraud- Bitdefender's cutting-edge filtering technology detects and alerts you to fraudulent websites.

  • Rescue Environment- Allows you to restart your computer within a specialized Rescue Environment to scan and remove advanced malware that cannot be removed while the operating system is running.

  • Antispam- Uses a combination of whitelist-blacklist, heuristic filter, and Bayesian learning module to keep your inbox spam-free. Bitdefender's anti-spam features work with any mailbox, including Outlook, Outlook Express, and Thunderbird.
