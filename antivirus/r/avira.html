SHORT ON TIME? HERE’S A QUICK SUMMARY

Avira is a German antivirus solution with a long history that dates back to 1986. Today, it protects over 500 million devices worldwide. Users like it for its simplicity and extensive features that include a VPN, password manager, web and email protection, file shredder, software updater, PC optimizer, and more. Of course, the main draw is definitely its award-winning antivirus scanner and the new anti-ransomware feature. However, with hundreds of antivirus solutions promising full-time protection, is Avira even worth a second look? Take a deep dive into this IT security company with our detailed review down below.

FEATURES

Avira Antivirus utilizes artificial intelligence, real-time scanner, and cloud technology to protect your computer against viruses, hackers, and spies. The cloud-based virus detector is a game-changer as it allows Avira to constantly update its lists of malware files and risky websites to combat new viruses. Once a threat is discovered, every device under the "cloud" is immediately secured. This happens instantaneously, so you won't have to worry about constantly updating your antivirus software to keep your defenses up-to-date. What's more, all the scanning, detection, and analysis happens in Avira's cloud server, not on your computer. This means the threat is eliminated before it even has a chance to infect your device!

Avira's real-time antivirus protection blocks not just viruses but also worms, trojans, spyware, adware, and ransomware. It also blocks malicious email attachments, phishing, spam, browser hijackers, and other unsafe web resources that may host malware or unwanted software. Avira also blocks botnets, DNS hijackers, banking Trojans, rootkits, and keyloggers to protect your identity, privacy, and banking information. With round-the-clock protection for your computer and files, you can explore the web confidently, download files without worry, or play online games without risking a malware infection!


ARE THERE ANY EXTRAS?

If you're still unsure whether or not Avira is the right antivirus solution for you, take a look at the following additional features. Just bear in mind that not all features are available in every package.

  • Phantom VPN - this free VPN rivals the features of other great standalone VPN brands: AES-256 military-grade encryption, strict no-log policy, and more. Surf the web anonymously, avoid price discrimination, and download huge files faster than before. However, the free version is limited to 500MB, so if you often download or watch content online, just upgrade to the Pro version for $10 a month.

  • Password Manager - generate strong and unhackable passwords that are saved in secure cloud storage. This is safer than entrusting your passwords in your browser, which may be prone to vulnerabilities. Having a master password also means that you won't have to memorize a dozen passwords. In addition, the autofill feature makes logging into your favorite websites fast, secure, and easy!

  • Privacy Pal - available on Windows OS, this tool helps dispose of confidential files, block third-party data collection, and get rid of digital breadcrumbs that cybercriminals can use to target you.

  • Safe Shopping- shopaholics will love this smart browser add-on that finds the best deals, better prices, discounts, and coupon codes from trusted shops and websites.

  • Software Updater - keep your applications up-to-date and safe from threats in a single click.

  • Browser Safety - this extension is designed to block ads, malicious sites, phishing and spam attacks, and other unwanted applications. Available on Chrome, Firefox, and Opera, Browser Safety will also alert you if you can't visit a website safely or privately.

  • Mobile Security - this mobile version of Avira has all the tools you need to protect your privacy and your smartphone/tablet. It includes a free VPN, software updater, anti-theft, photo cleaner, smartphone optimizer, webcam and mic protection, and many more.

  • System Speedup - is a free tuneup tool that cleans out the junk so your computer boots faster and runs smoother. Upgrade to the Pro version if you want over 40 tools, including battery optimization, registry cleaner, and game booster pro.

    EASE OF USE AND SETUP

    Installing Avira is a cakewalk that probably took 5 minutes tops. After purchasing your desired plan, you'll receive an email with a link "Install and activate now." Just click on the link, create your Avira account, and install the file. The dashboard is also pretty self-explanatory, with no more than four primary tabs: Status, Security, Privacy, and Performance. Even if you haven't used antivirus software before, you'll easily find your way around this program. The default setting should work just fine, but if you're a more advanced user, go ahead and customize your settings.


    WILL IT SLOW DOWN MY COMPUTER?

    Thanks to its cloud-based antivirus scanner, Avira has one of the smallest system footprints in the industry. It does its job in the cloud, freeing up your resources for work, entertainment, communication, and other online activities.


    PRICING AND PLANS

    Avira currently has three paid plans: Avira Antivirus Pro, Avira Security, and Avira Prime. We're honestly not too keen on the first two plans because each covers only one device. Prices range between $40.99 to $69.99. For a single device, it's certainly not cheap, so you might as well get Avira Prime to protect up to 5 devices. Considering all the extra features, however, the price is reasonably justified. Fortunately, Avira runs seasonal promotions, and right now, there's a special deal on Avira Prime. Use our link to purchase the most comprehensive plan at a 40% discount!


    CUSTOMER SUPPORT

    While it's pretty understandable, I find it slightly off-putting that you need to have an Avira license (in short, a subscriber) before you can make a support request. I usually test the responsiveness of a vendor's support team before deciding to purchase a software product. After all, what happens if I encounter a technical issue and support turns out to be pretty much non-existent? Turns out, my fears were unfounded. Once logged in, you'll have access to email and phone support. As per our experience, the support team has been pretty responsive and considerate. There's no live chat, but Prime subscribers get VIP customer assistance. Call via a toll-free number for priority access to Avira's IT specialists.


    THE VERDICT

    After looking at Avira from all angles, we can say that it is indeed one the best in the industry. On the surface, it may come across as pricey, but dig deeper, and you'll see that you'll get your money's worth, and then some. It has exceeded our expectations of what a stellar antivirus program should be and has blown us away with all the extra security and privacy tools. It's a cross-platform, multi-device antivirus solution that also boasts a user-friendly interface and reliable support.
